DA Hestrin announces 2022 PRIDE Award recipients

March 13, 2023

COUNTYWIDE – Each year, the Riverside County District Attorney’s Office recognizes the outstanding work done by its employees with its annual PRIDE Awards.

 

PRIDE, the Program Rewarding Incentive of District attorney Employees, honors attorney, and non-attorney staff for demonstrating extraordinary responsibility, professional development, productivity, dedication, initiative, a positive attitude, quality of work, dependability, cooperation, attendance, and public demeanor. We also recognize heroes in our community.

 

The 2022 PRIDE Awards banquet was held the evening of March 10, 2023, at the Windmill Canyon Ranch in Beaumont.

 

“Each day, the employees of the District Attorney’s Office work relentlessly in the pursuit of justice for crime victims across Riverside County,” DA Mike Hestrin said. “Those we are recognizing with PRIDE Awards have truly excelled throughout the 2022 calendar year.”

DA Mike Hestrin Announces the 2024 Justice Awards Recipients

March 3, 2025

COUNTYWIDE – The Riverside County District Attorney's Office celebrated its annual awards banquet on Friday, February 28, 2025. The event honored staff members’ outstanding achievements and unwavering dedication to justice and service within the community.

"Each day, the dedicated professionals of the District Attorney's Office strive to ensure that justice prevailed for the victims we served throughout Riverside County,” said DA Mike Hestrin. “The honorees of this year’s Justice Awards exemplified the highest standards of integrity and service, demonstrating a profound commitment to making our community safer.”

The 2024 Justice Awards banquet, formally called Excellence in Justice, took place at the Palm Springs Air Museum in Palm Springs. The cost of the banquet was covered by funds raised through office-led fundraisers and the generous support of local sponsors.

Image

Misdemeanor Prosecutor of the Year: Emily Stowe

Paralegal of the Year: Sue Wesolowski

Investigator of the Year: Claudia Estrada

Victim Services Specialist of the Year: Karla Melendrez

Administration Employee of the Year: Melina Perez-Ramirez

Clerical Employee of the Year: Becky Jewett

Clerical Employee of the Year: Cathryne Flores

Community Hero of the Year: Yvonne Bennett

Countywide DA Achievement/Leadership Award: Race-Blind Filing Team

Countywide Group Award: Communications Division

Admin. Region Group Award: Human Resources Unit

Eastern Region Group Award: Sexual Assault and Child Abuse Integrated Trial Team

Mid-County Region Group Award: Domestic Violence Integrated Trial Team

Southwest Group Award: Sexual Assault and Child Abuse Integrated Trial Team

Western Region Group Award: Clerical in the Sexual Assault and Child Abuse and Domestic Violence Integrated Trial Teams

As the event concluded, District Attorney Mike Hestrin expressed his gratitude to all employees for their relentless dedication and invaluable contributions, reaffirming the office’s mission to maintain justice and serve the community members of Riverside County.

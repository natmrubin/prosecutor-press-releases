State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle to Receive the Golden Eagle Award
Miami, Florida
, State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle will receive the Golden Eagle Award from the South Florida Hispanic Chamber of Commerce (SFLHCC) at their 31st Annual Installation Lunch & Golden Eagle Award Presentation being held on May 2, 2025, at the Biltmore Hotel.
The Golden Eagle Award is a prestigious honor presented by the SFLHCC to recognize excellence. The award is given to individuals that demonstrate outstanding creativity, professionalism, and impact in their work. Recipients of the Golden Eagle Award are chosen based on their contributions to advancing the message of faith, values, and integrity in the Hispanic community. The award is a symbol of distinction within the community and is highly coveted by those in the industry.
The event will also include a panel discussion titled “Understanding Miami-Dade County’s New Constitutional Offices”. The panelists will include Miami-Dade Sheriff Rosie Cordero-Stutz, Miami-Dade County Supervisor of Elections Alina Garcia, Miami-Dade County Property Appraiser Tomás Regalado, and Miami-Dade County Tax Collector Dariel Fernandez.
WHO:
State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle, Eleventh Judicial Circuit of Florida - Sheriff Rosie Cordero-Stuz, Miami-Dade Sheriff's Office - Alina Garcia, Miami-Dade County Supervisor of Elections - Tomás Regalado, Miami-Dade County Property Appraiser - Dariel Fernandez, Miami-Dade County Tax Collector
WHAT:
South Florida Hispanic Chamber of Commerce 31st Annual Installation Lunch & Golden Eagle Award Presentation
WHEN:
May 2, 2025 11:30- 2pm
WHERE:
Biltmore Hotel - 1200 Anastasia Avenue, Coral Gables, FL 33134

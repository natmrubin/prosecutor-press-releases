13 Charged In Round Up Of Major Catalytic Converter Fencing And Theft Ring
Miami, Florida,
State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle and Miami-Police Director Stephanie V. Daniels will hold a press conference to announce the arrest of 13 individuals involved in a criminal racketeering enterprise that deals in the theft, sale and buying of catalytic converters.
WHO:
Katherine Fernandez Rundle, State Attorney
Miami-Dade Police Director Stephanie Daniels
Miami-Dade Public Safety Chief James Reyes
WHAT:
Press Conference
WHEN:
Thursday, August 29, 2024 at 2:30 p.m.
WHERE:
Miami-Dade State Attorney’s Office
1350 N.W. 12th Avenue, Room S-418
PHOTO/INTERVIEW:
Miami-Dade Police Director Stephanie Daniels
#####

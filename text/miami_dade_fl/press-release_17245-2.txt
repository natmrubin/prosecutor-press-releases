MEDIA ADVISORY: Press Conference 4 Charged in 2021 El Mula Shootings
Miami, Florida
– State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle, Miami-Dade County Mayor Levine Cava, and Miami-Dade Police Department Deputy Director Christopher Carothers will hold a press conference to announce the arrest of 4 individuals charged in the 2021 shootings at the El Mula Banquet Hall.
WHO:
State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle
Miami-Dade County Mayor Levine Cava
Miami-Dade Police Department Director Stephanie V. Daniels
WHAT:
Press Conference
WHEN:
Thursday, June 13, 2024 – 2:00 PM
WHERE:
State Attorney’s Office, 1350 NW 12 Avenue, Miami, 33136, Room S418
#####
For further information, please contact Ed Griffith, Public Information Officer, at
(305) 547-0535
or
[email protected]

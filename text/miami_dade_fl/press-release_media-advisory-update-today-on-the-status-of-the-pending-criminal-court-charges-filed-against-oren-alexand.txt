Update Today on the Status of the Pending Criminal Court  Charges Filed Against Oren Alexander & Alon Alexander
Due to the volume of requests for court status information, State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle felt there was a need to provide the following information.
Oren Alexander:
• Criminal Court Case Number
F24-25503A
. Held no bond on Motion for Pretrial Detention. The motion is set on  the calendar for tomorrow in the courtroom of Judge Lody Jean. Tomorrow, the date to hear the motion (in the next 5 days) will be determined.
• Criminal Court Case Number
F24-25504
: $25,000 bond with house arrest and GPS monitor, stay away order from victim, surrender passports (standard bond is $15,000)
• Case Number
F24-25507
: $25,000 bond with house arrest and GPS monitor, stay away order from victim, surrender passports (standard bond is $15,000)
Alon Alexander:
Criminal Court Case Number
F24-25503B
: Held no bond on Motion for Pretrial Detention. The motion is set on the calendar for tomorrow in the courtroom of Judge Lody Jean. Tomorrow, the date to hear the motion (in the next 5 days) will be determined.

Condo President Charged in Aventura Condo Association Thefts
Miami, Florida
, State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle and Aventura Police Chief Michael Bentolila and City of Aventura Mayor Howard S. Weinberg will hold a press conference to announce the arrest of the President of the Turnberry On The Green Condominiums for alleged thefts from the condominium association.
WHO:
State Attorney Katherine Fernanez Rundle
Mayor Howard S. Weinberg, Mayor of City of Aventura
Aventura Police Chief Michael Bentolila
WHAT:
Press Conference
WHEN:
Tuesday, October 1, 2024 — 2:30 PM
WHERE:
State Attorney’s Office, 1350 NW 12 Avenue, Miami, 33136, Room S418
#####
For further information, please contact Ed Griffith, Public Information Officer, at
(305) 547-0535

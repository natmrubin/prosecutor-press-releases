MEDIA ADVISORY
For Immediate Release
October 10, 2006
Contact:
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
(714) 347-8405 Office
(714) 323-4486 Cell
WHO:    David Shoutyh Hwang, 35, the husband of a husband-and-wife team from Rancho Santa Margarita, charged with kidnapping with the intent to molest, multiple counts of producing child pornography, and molesting a toddler.  A trunk of tapes containing child pornography was found in their home.  A victim as young as eight-months was on the video tapes.
WHAT:   Is expected to enter a guilty plea for all 60 counts and be sentenced to 50 years to life.
WHEN:    Wednesday, October 11, 2006
WHERE: Department C37, Central Justice Center, 700 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana

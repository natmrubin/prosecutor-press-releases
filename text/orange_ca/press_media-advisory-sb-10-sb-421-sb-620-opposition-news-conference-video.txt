MEDIA ADVISORY – SB 10, SB 421, SB 620 Opposition news conference video
Date: July 11, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a video of yesterday’s news conference with Dr. Henry T. Nicholas III (Marsy’s Law), and Erin Runnion (The Joyful Child Foundation). They discussed strong opposition to three California State Senate bills currently pending in the Assembly that will make dramatic, negative changes to firearm sentencing enhancements, the bail system/ pretrial release, and sex offender registration.
To watch the video, please visit the following link:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S1TYbhu4aQc

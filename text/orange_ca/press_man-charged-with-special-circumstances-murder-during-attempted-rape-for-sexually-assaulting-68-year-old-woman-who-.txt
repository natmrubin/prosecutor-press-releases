SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was charged with special circumstances murder during attempted rape after the 68-year-old victim died due to injuries sustained in the assault.

Oct. 13, 2017, 10:00 a.m.
Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
On Sept. 10, 2017, at approximately 5:00 a.m., Varela is accused of attacking 68-year-old Jane Doe, whom he did not know, outside of a home in Garden Grove while she was on a walk.
The defendant is accused of assaulting the victim and attempting to rape her.
A witness called 911 and the Garden Grove Police Department (GGPD) arrested Varela at the scene.
The victim was transported to a local hospital and the defendant was initially charged with felony attempted murder and assault with intent to commit rape.
On Sept. 21, 2017, the victim died as a result of injuries sustained during the assault.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Cynthia Herrera, Homicide Unit

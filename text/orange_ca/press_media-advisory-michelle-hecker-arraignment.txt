For Immediate Release

July 10, 2008
Susan Kang Schroeder

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Michelle Hecker, 31, Newport Beach
WHAT: To be arraigned on charges of aiding and abetting her fiancé by assisting him to hide and maintain an unlawful sexual relationship with a 15-year-old girl.
WHEN: This afternoon, Thursday, July 10, 2008, at 1:30 p.m.
The defendant surrendered this morning to the Newport Beach Police Department.
WHERE: Department H-1 or H-2, Harbor Justice Center, Newport Beach

www.orangecountyda.com

MEDIA ADVISORY – Chief of Staff Susan Kang Schroeder on K-WAVE to discuss Strike Out Slavery
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: August 30, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
Chief of Staff Susan Kang Schroeder of the Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA)
WHAT:
Will be featured on K-WAVE 107.9 radio with Open Gate International CEO Deidre Pujols to discuss how the OCDA and the Orange County Human Trafficking Task Force has partnered with Strike Out Slavery to educate the public on the prevalence and dangers of human exploitation and trafficking.
WHEN:
Thursday, Aug. 31, 2017, at 9:30 a.m.
Strike Out Slavery awareness festival will take place on
Saturday, Sept. 16, 2017
. This special event will begin at 4:00 p.m. at Angel Stadium of Anaheim. After the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim play the Texas Rangers, the evening will conclude with fireworks and a rock concert. To learn more about the event or purchase tickets, click here:
https://www.strikeoutslavery.com/

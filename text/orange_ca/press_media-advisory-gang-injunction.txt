MEDIA ADVISORY – GANG INJUNCTION
For Immediate Release
November 16, 2006
Contact:
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
(714) 347-8405 Office
(714) 323-4486 Cell
MEDIA ADVISORY
*A video testimonial from concerned citizens will be available
WHAT:
Hearing and press conference regarding the second gang injunction to be signed in Orange County. The injunction is against 77 members of an Anaheim criminal street gang.
EVENT: Tomorrow, Friday, November 17, 2006
Hearing
will be held at
8:00 a.m.
in Department C-1, Central Justice Center, 700 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA.
Press conference
will be held at
10:00 a.m.
in the Law Library of the Office of the District Attorney, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA.
Please be advised that due to security issues, no one will be admitted to the press conference without presenting valid press credentials or a government or law enforcement ID.

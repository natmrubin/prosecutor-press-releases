MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Jill Bjorkholm Easter, 40, Irvine
WHAT: Pleaded guilty today to one felony count of false imprisonment by violence or deceit. She was sentenced to one year in jail and three years of formal probation.
Jill Easter’s co-defendant and husband Kent Wycliff Easter, 40, Irvine, is charged with one felony count each of conspiracy to falsely charge a crime, false imprisonment by violence or deceit, and conspiracy to commit a crime. He faces a maximum sentence of three years in state prison if convicted. Opening statements in the jury trial are anticipated to begin sometime next week.
WHEN: Today, Wednesday, Oct. 30, 2013
WHERE: Department C-34, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

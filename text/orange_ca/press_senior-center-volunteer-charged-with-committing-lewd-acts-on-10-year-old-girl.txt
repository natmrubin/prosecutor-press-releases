SENIOR CENTER VOLUNTEER CHARGED WITH COMMITTING LEWD ACTS ON 10-YEAR-OLD GIRL
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A senior center volunteer was charged today for committing lewd acts on a 10-year-old girl.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Francisco Tox Ramirez, 74, Santa Ana
Charged today with the following felony counts:
(3) Lewd act upon a child under 14
12 years in state prison
Arraignment
Aug. 27, 2018, 10:00 a.m.
Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
At the time of the crimes, Ramirez was a volunteer at the Southwest Senior Center in Santa Ana.
On July 6, 2018, the defendant is accused of committing lewd acts upon 10-year-old Jane Doe.
Witnesses reported to Jane Doe’s parents. Ramirez is accused of fleeing the scene.
Santa Ana Police Department (SAPD) investigated this case. SAPD later identified and arrested the defendant on Aug 24, 2018.
Anyone who believes they have been a victim is encouraged to contact Supervising Investigator Eric Wiseman at 714-347-8794
Prosecutor: Deputy District Attorney Kristin Bracic, Sexual Assault Unit

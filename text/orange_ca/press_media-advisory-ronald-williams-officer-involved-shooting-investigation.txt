MEDIA ADVISORY – RONALD WILLIAMS OFFICER-INVOLVED-SHOOTING INVESTIGATION
For Immediate Release

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a letter containing the investigation findings and legal conclusions regarding the Irvine Police Department’s officer-involved-shooting of Ronald Williams. The full letter, “OCDA Report Officer-Involved-Shooting Investigation – Ronald Williams,” is available at
www.orangecountyda.com
by selecting Investigation Letters under the Media Center pull-down menu.

News Conference Announcing Charges in “Among Heroes” Mural Vandalism
Case # TBA
Date: May 28, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:        Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas, City of Santa Ana Mayor Pro Tem Michele Martinez, and Santa Ana Police Department Chief David Valentin
WHAT:      Will host a news conference announcing charges against a defendant who is accused of vandalizing the mural “Among Heroes,” a memorial depicting nearly 200 portraits of Mexican-American men and women who served in the U.S. Military in World War II, the Korean War, and the Vietnam War.
WHEN:      Tuesday, May 29, 2018, at 2:00 p.m.
WHERE:   902 E. Washington Avenue, Santa Ana, CA 92701

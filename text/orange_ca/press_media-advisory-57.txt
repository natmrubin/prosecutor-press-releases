MEDIA ADVISORY
For Immediate Release
August 11th, 2005
Contact:
Mark Macaulay
Media Relations Spokesperson
(714) 347-8405
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:      Jennifer Henderson Deleon, 24, of Long Beach,  Skylar James Deleon, 26, of Long Beach,  Myron Gardner Sr., 42, of Long Beach,  John F. Kennedy, 39, of Long Beach, and Alonso Machain, 21, of Pico Rivera
WHAT:     Preliminary hearing to discuss the evidence linking the defendants to the deaths of Thomas and Jackie Hawks.  The Hawks were living on their 55-foot yacht “Well Deserved” in Newport Beach when they went missing on November 15, 2004.  Senior Deputy District Attorney Matt Murphy is prosecuting this case.
WHEN:     8:30 AM, Friday August 12th, 2005
WHERE: Department C5, Central Justice Center, 700 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701

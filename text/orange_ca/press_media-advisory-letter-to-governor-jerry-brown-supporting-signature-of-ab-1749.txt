Media Advisory – Letter to Governor Jerry Brown Supporting Signature of AB 1749
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: September 24, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a letter to Governor Jerry Brown expressing his gratitude for signing AB 1749, which clarifies workers’ compensation insurance benefits eligibility for peace officers injured out of state while performing defined law enforcement duties. The full letter, “
Letter to Governor Jerry Brown AB 1749
,” is available at
www.orangecountyda.org
by selecting Letters under the Reports pull-down menu.
WHEN: Today, Sept. 24, 2018

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Grisel Ramirez, 48, Garden Grove
WHAT:
Will be arraigned today for kidnapping a newborn baby from Garden Grove Medical Center after searching multiple hospital rooms in two different hospitals for a baby to steal.
Ramirez has been charged with one felony count of kidnapping of a child under 14 years old and five felony counts of first degree burglary.
If convicted, the defendant faces a maximum sentence of 17 years and eight months in state prison. The People will be requesting the defendant be held on $1 million bail.
WHEN: Today, Wednesday, Aug. 8, 2012, at 1:30 p.m.
WHERE: Department W-2, West Justice Center, Westminster
*Additional information to follow

Media Advisory – Gerald Willitis automobile auction
Date: August 28, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: The Orange County District Attorney Public Administrator’s Office (OCDAPA)
WHAT: Will be hosting an auction of Buena Park resident Gerald Willits’ collection of 69 cars. Willitis died in August 2014 and there is a will contest that will determine who inherits the proceeds of the sales. Willitis’ collection of vehicles range from 1930s Ford Model A Roadsters to a 1965 Volkswagen van and an experimental aircraft. Ken Porter Auctions of Carson will be conducting the auction. To view the automobiles up for auction visit the link:
https://bit.ly/1NM9RD9
. All inquires can be made to Ken Porter Auctions.
WHEN:
Monday
,
Aug. 31, 2015
, from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., viewing of the available vehicles.
Tuesday
,
Sept. 1, 2015
, 10:00 a.m., auction of vehicles. Gates are scheduled to open at 8:00 a.m.
WHERE: The OCDAPA parking lot at 1300 South Grand Avenue, Santa Ana, 92705.

CHIROPRACTOR CHARGED WITH SECRETLY VIDEOTAPING PEOPLE USING THE RESTROOM IN HIS OFFICE
FULLERTON – Chiropractor Won Joon Son, 34, Buena Park, was charged previously and arraigned yesterday on three counts of using a concealed camera secretly videotaping people using the bathroom in his chiropractic office located in Buena Park.   Son will be back at the North Justice Center, in Fullerton, on March 17th for pretrial and March 30th for jury trial.  He is out on $20,000 bail. These acts are misdemeanors and Son could be sent to jail for 18 months if convicted of all counts.
In November of 2005, Son is accused of concealing a video camera in the unisex restroom of his chiropractic office. He is accused of secretly videotaping three people while they were using the restroom. The camera was discovered by one of the victims.
Deputy District Attorney Steve Schriver is prosecuting this case.

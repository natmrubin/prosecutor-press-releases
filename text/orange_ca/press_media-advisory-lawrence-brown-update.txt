MEDIA ADVISORY
*A recording and photos of one of Brown’s victims will be available to the media
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas, Tustin Police Chief Scott Jordan, Santa Ana Police Chief Paul Walters, Senator Mimi Walters, Parole Administrator for Region IV Lindon Lewis, and a representative from Senator Lou Correa’s office.
WHAT: Will hold a press conference to provide updates in the case of convicted sex offender Lawrence Joseph Brown, 52.
WHEN:
Today, Tuesday, Jan. 18, 2011, at 12:45 p.m.
WHERE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office, first floor law library, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Please Note: No additional information or interviews will be available from any of the participating agencies until the press conference.

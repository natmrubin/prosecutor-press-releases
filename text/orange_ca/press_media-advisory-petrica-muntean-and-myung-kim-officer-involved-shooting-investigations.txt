MEDIA ADVISORY – PETRICA MUNTEAN AND MYUNG KIM OFFICER-INVOLVED-SHOOTING INVESTIGATIONS
For Immediate Release
August 7, 2014
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published two letters containing the investigation findings and legal conclusions regarding the Santa Ana Police Department’s officer-involved-shooting of Petrica Muntean and the Buena Park Police Department’s officer-involved-shooting of Myung Kim. The full letters, “OCDA Report Officer-Involved-Shooting Investigation – Petrica Muntean,” and “OCDA Report Officer-Involved-Shooting Investigation – Myung Kim,” are available at
www.orangecountyda.com
by selecting Investigation Letters under the Media Center pull-down menu.
WHEN: Today, Aug. 7, 2014

Canceled – MEDIA ADVISORY – Andrew Urdiales verdict
CANCELED
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Case # 09ZF0079
Date: May 23, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Andrew Urdiales, 53
WHAT:
A verdict will be read
in the jury trial of Urdiales, who is charged with murdering five Southern California women between 1986 and 1995. The OCDA is prosecuting a consolidated case on behalf of the Orange, Riverside, and San Diego County victims. Senior Deputy District Attorneys Matt Murphy and Eric Scarbrough of the Homicide Unit are prosecuting this case.
District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will be available for interviews with credentialed members of the press immediately following the verdict.
WHEN: Wednesday, May 23, 2018, at 10:30 a.m.
WHERE: Second floor lobby area, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Tagged with:

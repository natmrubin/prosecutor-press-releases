Circumstances of the Case
Taylor is accused of meeting 17-year-old Jane Doe 4 online, then forcibly raping and sodomizing the victim in August 2015.
In August 2016, the defendant is accused of again sexually assaulting then-18-year-old Jane Doe 4.
During the fall of 2017, Taylor is accused of having unlawful sexual intercourse with 14-year-old Jane Doe 2.
On March 11, 2018, Taylor is accused of having unlawful sexual intercourse with 16-year-old Jane Doe 3, then forcibly raping 15-year-old Jane Doe 1.
Jane Doe 1 reported the assault to a family member, who contacted the police.
Tustin Police Department investigated this case with the Orange County Sheriff’s Department and arrested the defendant on Nov. 16, 2018.

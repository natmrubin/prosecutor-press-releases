IRVINE MAN CHARGED WITH THREATENING TO SET OFF BOMB AT ORANGE COUNTY SHERIFF’S DEPARTMENT
AND ORANGE COUNTY JAIL
SANTA ANA, Calif. – An Irvine man was charged today with threatening to set off a bomb at the Orange County Sheriff’s Department (OCSD) and the Orange County Jail (OCJ).
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Pejman Abdolali Zanjani, 39, Irvine
Charged with the following felony counts
(2) False bomb report
Three years and eight months in state prison
Pre-trial hearing
Aug. 2, 2018, 9:00 a.m.
Department C-55, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
On July 19, 2018, Zanjani is accused of calling the OCSD non-emergency line and yelling profanities at the dispatcher.
During the call, Zanjani is further accused of threatening to set off a bomb at OCJ.
On July 21, 2018 the defendant is accused of calling OCSD a second time and threatening to blow up their office.
OCSD investigated this case and arrested Zanjani in his home.
Prosecutor: Deputy District Attorney Devin Campbell, Special Prosecutions Unit

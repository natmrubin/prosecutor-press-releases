MEDIA ADVISORY – TERRY SHIELDS ARRAIGNMENT
For Immediate Release
November 15, 2006
Contact:
Susan Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
(714) 347-8408 Office
(714) 292-2718 Cell
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Terry Shields, 51, Buena Park
WHAT: Will be arraigned on multiple charges of child molest, kidnapping, and child pornography production involving three girls under the age of 11.
WHEN: Tomorrow morning, November 16, 2006
WHERE: Department C-5, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana.
* For a copy of the original press release on the charges filed against Mr. Shields, please visit our website at
www.orangecountyda.com
and select “Press Releases/Media Advisories”.  The release was issued November 2, 2006 and is titled “School Bus Driver Charged with Molesting Three Girls”

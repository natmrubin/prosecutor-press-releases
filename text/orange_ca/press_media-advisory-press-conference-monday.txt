MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the press conference
WHO:
Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Todd Spitzer
WHAT:
OCDA will hold a press conference in conjunction with the Federal Bureau of Investigation to discuss the sentencing of convicted bomb maker Saleh Ali and its impact on public safety.
WHEN:
Monday, March 25, 2019, immediately following the sentencing.
Sentencing is scheduled for 8:30 a.m. in Department C-39, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana.
WHERE:
Second floor of the Central Justice Center by the flagpoles, 700 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701

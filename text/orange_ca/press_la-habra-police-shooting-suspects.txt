LA HABRA POLICE SHOOTING SUSPECTS
For Immediate Release
April 12th, 2005
Contact:
Mark Macaulay
Media Relations Spokesperson
(714) 347-8405
WHO:     David Mosqueda, 30, of Los Angeles and Andrew Gonzalez, 28, of Inglewood
WHAT:  Set to be arraigned on eleven felony counts including attempted murder, assault with a firearm on a peace officer, street terrorism, possession of a firearm with prior enumerated offense, and gang member carrying a loaded firearm in public.  If convicted on all counts, the defendants face between 25 years up to multiple life terms in state prison.  Senior Deputy District Attorney Jerry Schaffer in the Gang unit has been assigned to prosecute the case.
WHEN:   1:30 PM, Wednesday April 13, 2005
WHERE: Department N7 or N8, North Justice Center, 1275 North Berkeley Avenue, Fullerton, CA 92832.

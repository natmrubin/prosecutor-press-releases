OFFICE OF THE ORANGE COUNTY DISTRICT ATTORNEY SPONSORS OUTREACH BOOTH AT CINCO DE MAYO FESTIVAL IN ANAHEIM
Santa Ana – May 1, 2003 – The Orange County District Attorney’s Government and Community Relations Unit will be hosting an outreach booth at the annual Cinco de Mayo Festival this Saturday and Sunday (May 3rd and 4th), at La Palma Park, located at the corner of Harbor Boulevard and La Palma in Anaheim.
Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and OCDA staff and volunteers will be available this weekend to answer questions from the public and to hand out bilingual pamphlets and resource booklets about the criminal justice system and the role of the District Attorney’s Office in the Hispanic community of Orange County.
In addition to the informational materials, the OCDA booth will also feature prize drawings, free face painting, arts and crafts, and balloons.  District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will also be a special guest speaker at the Cinco de Mayo Festival on Sunday, May 4th at 10 a.m.
[Note: The Orange County District Attorney’s Government and Community Relations booth will be open from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday, May 3rd and Sunday, May 4th.  District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will be present at the outreach booth at 11 a.m. following his speech.]

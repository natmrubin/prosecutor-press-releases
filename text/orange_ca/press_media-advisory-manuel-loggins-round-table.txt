For Immediate Release

Susan Kang Schroeder

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will host a round-table meeting to discuss the investigation findings and legal conclusions regarding the Orange County Sheriff’s Department officer-involved-shooting involving Manuel Loggins.
WHEN: Today, Friday, Sept. 28, 2012, at 1:00 p.m.
WHERE: Law Library, Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Please Note:
Space is limited and RSVPs are required. The meeting is open to credentialed media only. Persons without both a confirmed reservation
and
media credentials will not be admitted.
Please RSVP to
Farrah.Emami@da.ocgov.com
no later than 12:00 p.m. today, Sept. 28, 2012.

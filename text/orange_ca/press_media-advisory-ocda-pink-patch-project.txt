MEDIA ADVISORY – OCDA Pink Patch Project
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: October 11, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and the OCDA Investigators’ Association (OCDAIA)
WHAT: OCDA is joining the “Pink Patch Project.” The Pink Patch Project is a public awareness campaign designed to bring attention to the fight against breast cancer and to support breast cancer research organizations. OCDAIA is selling limited edition pink OCDA police patches and pink ribbon lapel pins. All proceeds from these sales will be donated to breast cancer charity
Susan G. Komen-Orange County
.
WHEN: Sales begin today, Oct. 11, 2018
WHERE: To purchase the patch for $10 or the lapel pin for $5, please email
pinkpatch@da.ocgov.com
and we will contact you with further details.
For more information on this initiative, please visit
Pink Patch Project
.

Media Advisory – Video of News Conference announcing charges against Eric Cabrera Mora
Date: May 30, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a video of yesterday’s news conference with City of Santa Ana Councilman Juan Villegas, Santa Ana Police Department Chief David Valentin, and Veteran/community member Samuel Romero,
announcing charges
against Eric Alan Cabrera Mora for vandalizing a Santa Ana mural depicting Mexican-American war veterans who served in the U.S. Military. The defendant was also charged with tagging another business in Santa Ana.
To watch the video, please visit the following link:
https://youtu.be/1E4aoPVPiE0
WHEN: Today, May 30, 2018
Tagged with:

MEDIA ADVISORY
BAIL HEARING
WHO: Meshael Alayban, 42
WHAT: Will face a bail hearing for a forced labor human trafficking arrest. As the defendant poses a flight risk, the Orange County District Attorney’s (OCDA) Office will request that she be held without bail.
WHEN: Today, Wednesday, July 10, 2013, at 2:00 p.m.
WHERE: Department C-5, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
PRESS CONFERENCE
WHO: District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Irvine Police Chief David Maggard
WHAT: Will hold a press conference to discuss the case against Alayban.
WHEN: Immediately following the bail hearing.
WHERE: Second floor landing, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

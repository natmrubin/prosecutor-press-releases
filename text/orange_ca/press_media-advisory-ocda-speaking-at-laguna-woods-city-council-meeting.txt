MEDIA ADVISORY – OCDA speaking at Laguna Woods City Council meeting
Date: July 19, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will speak at a Laguna Woods City Council meeting to inform citizens about the rising issue of human trafficking in Orange County. District Attorney Rackauckas will also share what OCDA is doing to combat this problem in our county.
WHEN: Today, July 19, 2017, at 2:00 p.m.
WHERE: Laguna Woods City Hall, 24264 El Toro Road, Laguna Woods, California, 92637

MEDIA ADVISORY
SANTA ANA – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office has posted the video of this year’s Orange County Gang Reduction and Intervention Partnership (OCGRIP) and Chivas USA gang prevention incentive program, which kicked off on Wednesday, Feb. 9, 2012.
To view the video, visit the Media Center on the homepage at
www.orangecountyda.com
.
Internet Explorer will run the video directly through Windows Media. Certain Internet browsers, such as Firefox, Chrome, and Safari may require the video to be downloaded.
Any media or citizens who would like to receive press releases and media advisories from the District Attorney’s Office can sign up for e-mail notifications by visiting
www.orangecountyda.com
. Please select “E-Notifier Sign-up” on the homepage and follow the instructions.

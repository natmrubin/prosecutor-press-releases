MAN SENTENCED TO 70 YEARS IN PRISON FOR CONTINUOUSLY SEXUALLY ABUSING FOUR BOYS UNDER 14 YEARS OLD
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was sentenced to 70 years in state prison today for continuously sexually abusing four boys under 14 years old.
Defendant
Charges
Sentence
Uriel Ruiz Ricardez, 26, San Clemente
(4) Continuous sexual abuse
Lewd acts upon a child under 14
70 years in state prison
Mandatory lifetime sex offender registration
Circumstances of the Case
At the time of the crime, Ricardez was an acquaintance of the victims’ families.
Between August 2014 and September 2015, Ricardez committed lewd acts upon four boys under 14 years old on several occasions.
13-year-old John Doe 1 and 12-year-old John Doe 2’s parents reported the crime to OCSD, who investigated this case and arrested the defendant on Sept. 10, 2015.
11-year-old victims John Doe 3 and John Doe 4 were identified during the investigation following Ricardez’s arrest.
Prosecutor: Deputy District Attorney Courtney Thom, Sexual Assault Unit

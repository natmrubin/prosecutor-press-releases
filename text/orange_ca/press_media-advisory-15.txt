MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and California Department of Insurance Commissioner Steve Poizner
WHAT: Will hold a press conference on an undercover sting that netted over 11 defendants, primarily chiropractors and an attorney, who attempted to defraud insurance companies through an illegal patient referral scheme and over-billing for services not rendered.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Wednesday, Feb. 4, 2009, at 12:00 p.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, 92701

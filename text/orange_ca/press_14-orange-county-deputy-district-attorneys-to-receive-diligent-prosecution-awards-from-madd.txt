14 ORANGE COUNTY DEPUTY DISTRICT ATTORNEYS TO RECEIVE DILIGENT PROSECUTION AWARDS FROM MADD
District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will join the Orange County Chapter of Mothers Against Drunk Driving (MADD) to honor 14 members of the Orange County District Attorney’s office and 27 members of Orange County Law Enforcement for their outstanding efforts to protect the public from drunk drivers.
Time & Place: 12 PM, April 13, 2005, in the White House Room at The Richard Nixon Library, 18001 Yorba Linda Boulevard, Yorba Linda, CA 92886.

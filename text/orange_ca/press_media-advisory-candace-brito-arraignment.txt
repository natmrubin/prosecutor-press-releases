MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Candace Marie Brito, 27, Santa Ana
WHAT: Has been charged with one felony count of murder for her role in the death of 23-year-old Kim Pham in Santa Ana in the early morning hours of Jan. 19, 2014. She faces a maximum sentence of 15 years to life in state prison if convicted. The people will be requesting Brito be held on $1 million bail at her arraignment today.
WHEN: Today, Tuesday, Jan. 28, 2014, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
The investigation is ongoing and additional information will not be released.

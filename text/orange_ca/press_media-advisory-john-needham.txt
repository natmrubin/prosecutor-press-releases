MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: John Wylie Needham, 26
WHAT: The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) received confirmation today that murder defendant Needham died on Feb. 19, 2010, in Pima, AZ. The cause of Needham’s death is unknown and Arizona authorities are still investigating. The murder charges filed by the OCDA against the defendant on Sept. 2, 2008, will be dismissed.
For more information surrounding the details of Needham’s murder case please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
, and select the Sept. 2, 2008, press release under Press Releases/Media Advisories entitled “War Veteran Charged with Beating-murder of 19-year-old Female Friend in San Clemente.”

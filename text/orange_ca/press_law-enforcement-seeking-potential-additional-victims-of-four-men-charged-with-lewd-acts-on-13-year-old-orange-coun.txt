Circumstances of the Case
On Sept. 13, 2016, detectives from the Huntington Beach Police Department (HBPD) began an investigation into four adult males accused of communicating online with a minor victim, 13-year-old Jane Doe.
The defendants, who do not know each other, are accused of utilizing multiple social media platforms under false names to communicate with the victim between 2015 and 2016.
Three of the males are accused of traveling to Huntington Beach and Fountain Valley and engaging in sexual activity with Jane Doe.
On Jan. 18, 2018, after an extensive investigation, HBPD arrested Williams and Anaraki.
On Jan. 21, 2018, HBPD and Sacramento Police Department arrested Palmer.
On March 22, 2018, detectives from the Palm Beach Gardens Police Department took Castaldi into custody.
On April 16, 2018, HBPD extradited Castaldi back to California.
OCDA and HBPD believe there may be additional victims who might have been contacted or solicited online by these suspects. If you have further information or know of potential victims, please contact the Huntington Beach Police Information Hotline at 714-375-5066.  Anyone wishing to remain anonymous should call the WeTip Anonymous Crime Reporting Hotline at 1-800-78-CRIME.

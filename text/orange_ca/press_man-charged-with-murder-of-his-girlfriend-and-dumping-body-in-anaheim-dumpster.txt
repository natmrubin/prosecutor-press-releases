Date: November 2, 2018
MAN CHARGED WITH MURDER OF HIS GIRLFRIEND AND DUMPING BODY IN ANAHEIM DUMPSTER
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was charged today with the murder of his girlfriend and dumping the body in an Anaheim dumpster.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Amer Saleh Alhasan, 28, La Habra
Charged today, Nov. 2, 2018 with the following felony count:
Murder
25 years to life in state prison
Arraignment
Nov. 15 at 10:00 a.m.
Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
Prior to 2:00 a.m. on Oct. 30, 2018, Alhasan is accused of murdering his girlfriend Tiyanie Ly
At approximately 2:15 a.m. on Oct. 30, 2018, the victim’s body was discovered stuffed in a large duffle bag inside a dumpster in the 2100 block of S. Balboa Plaza of Anaheim.
On Oct. 31, 2018, Anaheim Police Department and the US Marshal Service arrested the defendant at the Los Angeles International Airport.
Alhasan was checked in for a flight to Germany, with a final destination of Jordan.
This case is still under investigation. No further information will be released at this time.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Janine Madera, Homicide Unit

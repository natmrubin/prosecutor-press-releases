MEDIA ADVISORY – STEVEN ANDREASON CUSTODIAL DEATH INVESTIGATION
Date: February 9, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a letter containing the investigation findings and legal conclusions regarding the Orange County Sheriff’s Department’s custodial death of Inmate Steven Andreason. The full letter, “OCDA Report Custodial Death Investigation – Inmate Steven Andreason,” is available at www.orangecountyda.org by selecting Officer-Involved Shootings and Custodial Death Letters under the Reports pull-down menu.
WHEN: Today, Feb. 9, 2016

Farrah Emami

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney’s Chief of Staff Susan Kang Schroeder
WHAT: Will attend the Buena Park City Council meeting today in support of a City Ordinance to create a child safety zone in city parks to further protect children from registered sex offenders. The Council will hold a study session during the meeting and discuss whether Buena Park should enact a City Ordinance similar to the County Ordinance proposed by Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Supervisor Shawn Nelson (Fourth District) and passed unanimously by the Orange County Board of Supervisors on April 5, 2011. The County Ordinance makes it a misdemeanor for registered sex offenders to enter County recreational areas where children regularly gather. Between May 2011 and February 2012, the Cities of Westminster, Irvine, La Habra, Los Alamitos, Huntington Beach, Yorba Linda, Laguna Hills, Lake Forest, Mission Viejo, Rancho Santa Margarita, and Seal Beach have passed similar ordinances.
WHEN: Tuesday, April 10, 2012, at 7:00 p.m.
WHERE: City Hall Council Chambers, 6650 Beach Boulevard, Buena Park
To read the full County Ordinance, please select the April 5, 2011, press release titled “Orange County Board of Supervisors Unanimously Votes to Adopt New Law to Keep Registered Sex Offenders Out of Parks, Harbors, Beaches and Playgrounds,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories at
www.orangecountyda.com
.
To read the agenda item, please select City Services from the homepage
www.buenapark.com
. From the pull down menu, select City Council and click on City Agenda to view the agenda for April 10, 2012.

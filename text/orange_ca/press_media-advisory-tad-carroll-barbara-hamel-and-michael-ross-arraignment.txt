MEDIA ADVISORY
*There is a warrant for the arrest of the third defendant Michael Ross
WHO: Tad Allen Carroll, 41, and Barbara Ann Hamel, 47
WHAT: Will be arraigned this morning for the special circumstances murder for financial gain of 55-year-old Chi Bui after following the victim home from Hawaiian Gardens Casino in Los Angeles. A warrant was issued for the arrest of the third defendant Michael Ross when charges were filed against Carroll and Hamel.
WHEN:
This morning
, Monday, Sept. 13, 2010.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana

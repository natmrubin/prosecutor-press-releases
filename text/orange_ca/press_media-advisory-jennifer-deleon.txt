MEDIA ADVISORY – JENNIFER DELEON
For Immediate Release
Case # 05HF0372
October 4, 2007
Contact:
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Jennifer Deleon, 26, of Long Beach
WHAT: To be sentenced for the 2004
Newport Beach murder-for-profit of Thomas and Jackie Hawks. The victims were handcuffed, tied to the anchor of their boat, and drowned so that Jennifer Deleon and her husband, Skylar Deleon, could steal the boat and take control of the Hawks’ bank account.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, October 5, 2007 at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-35, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

For Immediate Release
Case # 13WF2301
July 30, 2013
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Chase Scott Christman, 19, Simi Valley
WHAT: Is accused of participating in the Huntington Beach downtown disturbance on Main Street that occurred July 28, 2013, after a surfing contest had ended. Christman is charged with one felony count of vandalism, one misdemeanor count of inciting a riot, and one misdemeanor count of refusal to disperse. The People will be requesting bail be set at $20,000 at his arraignment today. If convicted, he faces a maximum sentence of three years in state prison.
WHEN: Today, Tuesday, July 30, 2013, at 1:30 p.m.
WHERE: Department W-1, West Justice Center, Westminster

MEDIA ADVISORY – Prosecutors from Korea Visit the OCDA
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: October 29, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:   Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: OCDA will be hosting 18 prosecutors from Korea for a full-day event at the Office. These prosecutors will have the chance to meet and learn from our prosecutors and investigators through topic specific presentations. They will conclude the day in court observing an ongoing trial and meeting with one of the Superior Court judges.
WHERE: Morning: OCDA Office, 401 W. Civic Center Dr., Santa Ana, CA 92701
Afternoon: Superior Court of Orange County, 700 W. Civic Center Dr., Santa Ana, CA 92701
WHEN: Thursday Nov. 1, 2018

MEDIA ADVISORY – BRETT THOMAS PAROLE HEARING INFORMATION
Date: April 16, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the press conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and Lynette Duncan, daughter and sister of Brett Matthew Paul Thomas’ murder victim Aubrey Duncan, and Dr. Henry T. Nicholas, III, brother of murder victim Marsy Nicholas and chief architect of Marsy’s Law
WHAT: Will hold a press conference tomorrow to discuss their opposition of parole at a hearing tomorrow in the Richard J. Donovan Correctional Facility in San Diego, for Thomas, a mass murderer who went on a 9-day killing spree in 1977 in Orange County. Pictures of crime scene and victims will be made available.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, April 17, 2015, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana

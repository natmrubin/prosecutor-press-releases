Media Advisory – OCDA Public Administrator honors deceased veterans
Date: November 11, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: The Orange County District Attorney Public Administrator’s Office
WHAT: Honored 16 deceased veterans who passed away indigent in 2015. The office of the Public Administrator has unique powers to secure real and personal property until an eligible administrator is appointed. The Public Administrator protects the assets and manages the affairs of deceased residents of Orange County, who at the time of death left no known heirs, no will, no named executor or an executor who is ineligible. The veterans listed below were issued certificates, signed by President Barack Obama, recognizing them for their service:
Kirby Callaway Horton
Richard Lowe Dick
Stephen Charles Moore
James Osborne Esterline
Kenneth Edward Cheek, Jr.
Gary Clifford Winter
John Bernard Wilkie
George Fredrick Fickeissen
Charles Louis Valentine, Jr.
David A. Brown
Richard Frederick Pillsbury
Karl Edward Giesler
Gustaf Arnold Hague
Thomas Alvin Coppenbarger
Conrad Edward Harm
Spencer Andrew Cole
WHEN: Today, Nov. 11, 2015

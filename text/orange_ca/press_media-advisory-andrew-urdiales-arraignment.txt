For Immediate Release
Case #09ZF0079
October 6, 2011
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408

Farrah Emami
Spokesperson

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Andrew Urdiales, 47
WHAT: Has arrived in California after being extradited from Illinois for murdering five Southern California women between 1986 and 1995 in Orange, Riverside, and San Diego Counties. Urdiales is expected to be arraigned tomorrow in Orange County on the charges.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, Oct. 7, 2011, at 1:30 p.m.
WHERE: Department C-5, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
To read the full press release on this case, visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the Oct. 4, 2011 press release titled, “Convicted Illinois Serial Murderer Extradited for Murdering Five Southern California Women Between 1986 and 1995” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

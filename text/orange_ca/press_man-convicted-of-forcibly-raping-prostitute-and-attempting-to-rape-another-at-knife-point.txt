MAN CONVICTED OF FORCIBLY RAPING A PROSTITUTE AND ATTEMPTING TO
RAPE
ANOTHER AT KNIFE-POINT
WESTMINSTER –
A man was found guilty by a jury today of forcibly raping one woman and attempting to rape another at knife point after picking them up as prostitutes and refusing to pay for sex.
Gilberto Cruz Silva, 30, Corona, was convicted of one felony count each of forcible rape, assault with the intent to commit rape, and criminal threats. The sentencing enhancements and allegations for the personal use of a deadly weapon and causing great bodily injury were found true. Silva faces a maximum sentence of 36 years to life in state prison at his sentencing on Jan. 21, 2011, at 1:30 p.m. in Department W-9, West Justice Center, Westminster.
At approximately 2:00 a.m. on Sept. 20, 2009, Silva picked up prostitute Jane Doe #1 in his 1997 green Chevrolet Astro Van on Harbor Boulevard in Santa Ana. The defendant drove with the victim to a nearby mobile home park and parked in a secluded area. Silva pulled out a knife and told Jane Doe #1 he wanted to have sex but had no money. Silva then forcibly raped the victim at knife point. Jane Doe #1 was able to flee. The victim went to the hospital and the Santa Ana Police Department (SAPD) was contacted.
A few hours later, at approximately 4:00 a.m., Silva picked up prostitute Jane Doe #2 in his van in the same area where he picked-up Jane Doe #1. The defendant drove with the victim to the same mobile home park and parked in a secluded area. Silva pulled out a knife and demanded the victim to have sex with him, forcing Jane Doe #2 to produce her own knife in self-defense. Silva engaged in a violent knife fight with the victim in the back of his van. Silva grabbed Jane Doe#2, head-butted her, and said “I’m going to kill you.” During the struggle the victim was able to escape.
Silva crashed into several parked cars while fleeing the scene. The defendant drove to his home in Corona and went to the hospital for his stab wounds sustained during the struggle with Jane Doe #2. Silva reported to local police that he was attacked by a prostitute. The SAPD was contacted.
Deputy District Attorney Robert Mestman of the Sexual Assault Unit is prosecuting this case.

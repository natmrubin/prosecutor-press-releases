DA Sponsors Outreach Booth at Santa Ana Festival
For Immediate Release
October 25th, 2001
Contact: Rocio G. Gonzalez (714) 943-0238
DA Sponsors Outreach Booth at Santa Ana Festival
SANTA ANA—The Orange County District Attorney’s Government and Community Relations unit will be hosting a booth at the Fiestas de Las Americas festival Saturday and Sunday in downtown Santa Ana.
District Attorney Tony Rackauckas, staff, and volunteers will be on hand to answer questions from the public and to disseminate bilingual pamphlets and resource booklets. The District Attorney’s Office hosts the booth to outreach and to educate the Hispanic community about the criminal justice system and the role of the District Attorney’s Office in their community. The booth will also feature prize drawings, free face painting, balloons and educational pamphlets.
The Government and Community Relations booth will be located on the corner of Fourth and Spurgeon streets from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday and Sunday. Rackauckas will be at the booth from 1 p.m. to 2 p.m. Saturday to meet members of the community and answer any questions that they may have.

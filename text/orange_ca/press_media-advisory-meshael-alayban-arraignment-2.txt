MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Meshael Alayban, 42
WHAT: A Saudi Arabian princess will be arraigned on one felony count of human trafficking of a Kenyan woman into the United States and forcing the victim to work as a domestic servant against her will in the first forced labor human trafficking case to be prosecuted in Orange County under California’s Proposition 35.
WHEN: Today, Thursday, July 11, 2013, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-5, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

Contact:

Office: 714-347-8415
SPECIAL FAMILIES CELEBRATE THE SEASON AT
THE 9
TH
ANNUAL OCDA ADOPT-A-FAMILY FESTIVITY
SANTA ANA – As part of an ongoing commitment to improve the quality of life in Orange County communities, the Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA), Delhi Community Center, and Toys for Tots will celebrate the season with 40 special families at the 9th Annual Adopt-a-Family event. The holiday party will be held tomorrow, Saturday, December 15, 2007 from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. at the
Delhi Community Center at 505 East Central Avenue, Santa Ana.
The Delhi Community Center selected 40 families needing some cheer during the holidays. They will receive holiday dinner gift certificates and toys for their children, which were donated by OCDA’s staff, family, friends, and affiliates. The adopted families are invited to attend a holiday celebration to receive their gifts and participate in a fun-filled morning featuring arts and crafts activities, balloons, baked goods, and an opportunity to meet and take pictures with Santa Claus.
A resource table will also be available with staff and volunteers on hand to answer questions about the criminal justice system and disseminate bilingual resources.

MEDIA ADVISORY – OPERATION STRAIGHT BODY
For Immediate Release
June 3, 2010
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will hold a press conference on over 50 defendants charged in Operation Straight Body following a 5-month undercover sting by the OCDA Automobile Insurance Fraud Unit targeting auto body repair facilities engaging in insurance fraud.
WHEN:
Today, Thursday, June 3, 2010, at 11:00 a.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, CA 92701

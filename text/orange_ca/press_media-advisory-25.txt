MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:  Tony Rackauckas, District Attorney of Orange County
WHAT:  Will be sworn in for his third term as District Attorney in the Oath of Office Ceremony.  Mr. Rackauckas will speak about his goals for the upcoming term.
WHEN:  Wednesday, January 10, 2007 at 5:00 p.m.
WHERE: The Orange County Pavilion, 801 N. Main Street, Santa Ana, CA 92701
Please RSVP by tomorrow, January 5, 2007 to Susan Kang Schroeder at (714) 347- 8408 or
susan.schroeder@da.ocgov.com

MEDIA ADVISORY – BENJAMIN GOLDEN CHARGES
Case # 15WM14655
Date: November 3, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Benjamin Allen Golden, 32, Newport Beach
WHAT: Was charged today with four misdemeanor counts, including assault on a public transportation property, battery on a public transit employee with injury, assault, and battery. If convicted, he faces a maximum sentence of one year in jail and a $10,000 fine. The People will be requesting he be held on $20,000 bail at his arraignment. The defendant is currently out of custody.
WHEN: Arraignment scheduled Nov. 17, 2015, at 8:30 a.m.
WHERE: Department W-3, West Justice Center, Westminster

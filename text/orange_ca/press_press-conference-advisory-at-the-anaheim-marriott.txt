PRESS CONFERENCE ADVISORY (AT THE ANAHEIM MARRIOTT)
WHO:      Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will release the results of a 10-year analysis of gang activity and cases in Orange County along with 2004 gang statistics.
WHAT: Release of the 2004 Orange County Gang Report at the National Gang Conference in Anaheim, CA.
WHEN: Thursday, July 21, 2005 at 10 AM
WHERE: Anaheim Marriott in the Gold Key conference room, 700 West Convention Way, Anaheim, CA 92802

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Peter Nong Le, 60, of Santa Ana.
WHAT: Set for arraignment on charges he murdered his sister-in-law and her teenage daughter. Senior Deputy District Attorney Scott Simmons in the Homicide unit has been assigned to prosecute the case.
WHEN: Friday, January 28, 2005, at 8:30 AM in department C55.
WHERE: Central Justice Center, 700 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701.

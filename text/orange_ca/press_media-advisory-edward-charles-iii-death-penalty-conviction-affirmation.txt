Media Advisory – Edward Charles III death penalty conviction affirmation
Case # 94NF2611
Date: June 2, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: The Supreme Court of California
WHAT: Affirmed the death penalty verdict of Edward Charles III for the 1994 stabbing, strangling, and beating murders of Edward Charles III’s brother, Danny Charles, mother, Dolores Charles, and father, Edward Charles II.
WHEN: Yesterday, June 1, 2015
WHERE: To read the Supreme Court’s opinion, visit
https://www.courts.ca.gov/opinions/documents/S076337.PDF

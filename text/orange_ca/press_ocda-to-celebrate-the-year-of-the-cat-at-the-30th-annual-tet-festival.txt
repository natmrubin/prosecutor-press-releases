OCDA TO CELEBRATE THE “YEAR OF THE CAT”
AT THE 30TH ANNUAL TET FESTIVAL
SANTA ANA – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) is proud to participate Saturday, Feb. 5, 2011, in the 30
th
Annual Tet Festival celebration of the “Year of the Cat” Vietnamese New Year.
The 2011 Tet Festival will be held at Garden Grove Park in Garden Grove and feature entertainment, games and prizes, food, art and culture vendors, and exhibitors from local business, government, and law enforcement agencies.
The OCDA Government and Community Relations Unit will host booth C-24 to speak with festival visitors and hand out information in English and Vietnamese to encourage community awareness regarding public safety and participation in law enforcement. The resource materials will cover information regarding victim resources, crime prevention, the criminal justice system, and the negative consequences of driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
The festival is organized by the Union of Vietnamese Student Associations. For more information on the Tet Festival, visit
https://tetfestival.org/
.

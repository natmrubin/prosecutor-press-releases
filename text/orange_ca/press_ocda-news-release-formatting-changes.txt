OCDA NEWS RELEASE FORMATTING CHANGES
SANTA ANA, Calif. – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) Public Affairs Unit will begin issuing some news releases in a new, condensed format to make pertinent information more quickly available. Please see the example below.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Name, Age, City of residence
Felony/ Misdemeanor counts
Sentencing enhancements
Time in state prison or county jail
Type of Hearing
Date and time
Location
Circumstances of the Case
The defendant is accused of the following:
The _____ Police Department investigated this case.
Prosecutor: Deputy District Attorney ______, ______ Unit

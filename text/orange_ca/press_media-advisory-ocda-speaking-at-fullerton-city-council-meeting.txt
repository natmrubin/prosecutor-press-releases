Media Advisory – OCDA speaking at Fullerton city council meeting
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: July 17, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will host an informational presentation to update citizens about the rising issue of human trafficking in Orange County. District Attorney Rackauckas will also share what the OCDA is doing to combat this problem in our county.
WHEN: Tonight, July 17, 2018, at 6:30 p.m.
WHERE: Fullerton City Hall, 303 W. Commonwealth Avenue, Fullerton, CA 92832
Tune in to watch on Facebook live at:
https://www.facebook.com/OCDATony

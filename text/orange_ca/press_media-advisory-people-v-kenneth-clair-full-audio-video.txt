MEDIA ADVISORY – People v. Kenneth Clair Full Audio video
Case # C-57572
Date: November 9, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a video in conjunction with the 7-part series regarding
People v. Kenneth Clair
, who is expected to be resentenced for the violent murder of Linda Rodgers in 1984. The video contains the full audio-recording of a conversation between defendant Clair and Pauline Flores as it was presented in evidence to the trial judge, jury, and all appellate courts.
This video series is being released in response to false information disseminated by inmate Clair and his defense team across the Internet. The video may be viewed at the following link:
https://youtu.be/k97TWr7AioQ
WHEN: Today, Nov. 9, 2016

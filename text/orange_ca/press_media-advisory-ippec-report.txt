MEDIA ADVISORY – IPPEC REPORT
Date: January 4, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information will be available prior to the roundtable
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will hold a roundtable today to discuss the Informant Policies & Practices Evaluation Committee (IPPEC) report released today in regards to the OCDA.
The report titled, “OCDA Informant Policies & Practices Evaluation Committee Report,” is available on the OCDA website at www.orangecountyda.org, under the Reports tab.
WHEN: Today, Monday, Jan. 4, 2016, at 3:00 p.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana

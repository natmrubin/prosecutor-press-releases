MAN TO BE ARRAIGNED FOR MURDERING MAN AND SHOOTING SECOND VICTIM OUTSIDE CLUB IN WESTMINSTER
*Second suspect charged with being an accessory after the fact
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man is scheduled to be arraigned today for murdering a man and shooting a second victim outside a club in Westminster. A second man was charged with being an accessory after the fact.

Circumstances of the Case
In the early morning hours on Oct. 28, 2017, Tran and Chau are accused of being at Club Bleu on Beach Blvd. in Westminster.
Tran is accused of firing multiple shots in the direction of 53-year-old Huy P. and his associate, 47-year-old John Doe, outside of the club at approximately 1:30 a.m.
Huy P. was shot in the head and was transferred to a local hospital where he was later pronounced dead as a result of the gunshot wound.
John Doe was shot in the lower body and survived his injuries.
Both defendants are accused of fleeing the scene after the shooting, and Chau is accused of driving Tran away.
The Westminster Police Department (WPD) investigated this case and arrested Chau on Oct. 31, 2017, near his residence in Fountain Valley.
Tran was arrested by WPD on Nov. 2, 2017, at 12:30 a.m. at his residence in Santa Ana.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Keith Burke, Homicide Unit

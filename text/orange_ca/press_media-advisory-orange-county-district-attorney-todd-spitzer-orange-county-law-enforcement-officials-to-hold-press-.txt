FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: May 15, 2023
Kimberly Edds
Director of Public Affairs
Office: 714-347-8405, Cell: 714-504-1917

Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer, Orange County Law Enforcement Officials
to Hold Press Conference on Residential and Commercial Burglary Eradication Efforts
SANTA ANA
– Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer and Orange County law enforcement officials will hold a press conference to make an important announcement on efforts to combat residential, commercial, and smash and grab burglaries.
WHO
:
Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer
Orange County Fourth District Supervisor Doug Chaffee
Orange County Sheriff’s Department Assistant Sheriff John McCulloch
Brea Police Chief Adam Hawley
Irvine Police Chief Michael Kent
WHEN
:
Wednesday, May 17, 2023
10 a.m.
WHERE
:
Orange County District Attorney’s Office
300 N. Flower Street, Santa Ana, California
First Floor Media Room
LIVESTREAM:
The press conference will be streamed live on Facebook at
www.facebook.com/ocdatoddspitzer

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and a representative from the Fullerton Police Department
WHAT: Will hold a press conference to announce the filing of charges against Andrew Thomas Gallo, 22, Riverside. Gallo is accused of killing three people, including Angel’s baseball player Nick Adenhart, and injuring a fourth in an early morning car crash while driving under the influence of alcohol.
WHEN:
This afternoon
, Friday, April 10, 2009 at 2:30 p.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, 92701

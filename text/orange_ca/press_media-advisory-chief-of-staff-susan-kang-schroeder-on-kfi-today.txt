MEDIA ADVISORY – CHIEF OF STAFF SUSAN KANG SCHROEDER ON KFI TODAY
For Immediate Release
June 27, 2012
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Chief of Staff Susan Kang Schroeder
WHAT: Will be speaking with KFI’s Bill Carroll about the defense audio tape released by defense attorney John Barnett in the OCDA case against two police officers from the Fullerton Police Department (FPD) for their criminal participation in the beating-death of a homeless man, 37-year-old Kelly Thomas. Officer Manuel Ramos, 38, is charged with one felony count of second degree murder and one felony count of involuntary manslaughter. Corporal Jay Cicinelli, 40, is charged with one felony count of involuntary manslaughter and one felony count of the use of excessive force.
WHEN: Today, Wednesday, June 27, 2012, at 12:20 p.m.
WHERE: The Bill Carroll Show, radio station KFI AM 640
For more information on the case, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the Sept. 11, 2011, press release titled, “OCDA Charges Two Fullerton Police Officers for the Beating-Death of 37-Year-Old Homeless Man,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

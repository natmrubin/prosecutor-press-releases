Media Advisory – Supervisor Do Residence
Date: October 5, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a letter containing the investigation findings and legal conclusions regarding a complaint received by the OCDA as to Supervisor Andrew Do’s residence status. The full letter, “Supervisor Andrew Do Residency Investigation,” is available at www.orangecountyda.org by selecting Letters under the Reports pull-down menu.
WHEN: Today, Oct. 5, 2015

MEDIA ADVISORY – Letter to Governor Jerry Brown Opposing Parole of Jeen Han
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Case #96HF1017
Date: November 27, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a letter to Governor Jerry Brown regarding the parole of Jeen “Gina” Han, who was convicted of conspiring to murder her twin sister in 1998. The Board of Parole Hearings granted parole to this inmate at her initial parole hearing on Oct. 31, 2017. Due to Han’s unreasonable risk of danger to society, the OCDA is requesting Governor Brown use executive power to reverse the Board’s decision.
The full letter, “Letter to Governor Jerry Brown Opposing Parole of Jeen Han,” is available at www.orangecountyda.org by selecting Letters under the Reports pull-down menu.
WHEN: Today, November 27, 2017

MEDIA ADVISORY – Andrew Urdiales penalty phase verdict
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Case # 09ZF0079
Date: June 12, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Andrew Urdiales, 53
WHAT: A penalty phase verdict will be read in the jury trial of Urdiales, who was convicted of murdering five Southern California women between 1986 and 1995. The Orange County District Attorney’s Office is prosecuting a consolidated case on behalf of the Orange, Riverside, and San Diego County victims. Senior Deputy District Attorneys Matt Murphy and Eric Scarbrough of the Homicide Unit are prosecuting this case.
WHEN: Wednesday, June 13, 2018, at 9:30 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-35, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Tagged with:

Who

Sarah Bench-Solario, who was a married, 28-year-old schoolteacher for
Santiago Middle School at the time of the offenses
What:
Sentencing for Bench-Solario convicted of 29 counts of molesting three boys ages 12 to 14 at the time of the molestation.
The boys were seduced by Bench-Salorio over dinner, through emails and phone calls for months, before the defendant would start molesting them.

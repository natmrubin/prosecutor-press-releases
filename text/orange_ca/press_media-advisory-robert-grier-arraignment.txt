MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Robert Vincent Grier, 26, Fremont
WHAT: Is charged with one felony count each of pimping and pandering and has a prior prison conviction for a 2008 robbery in Alameda County. The People will be requesting he be held on $250,000 bail. If convicted, he faces a maximum sentence of 12 years in state prison.
WHEN: Arraignment this afternoon. The time is to be determined.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
The investigation by Anaheim Police Department is ongoing. Deputy District Attorney Brad Schoenleben of the HEAT Unit is prosecuting this case.

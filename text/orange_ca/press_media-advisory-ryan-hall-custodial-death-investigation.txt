Date: December 1, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a letter containing the investigation findings and legal conclusions regarding the Orange County Sheriff’s Department custodial death of Inmate Ryan Hall. The full letter, “
OCDA Report Custodial Death Investigation – Inmate Ryan Hall
,” is available at www.orangecountyda.org by selecting Officer-Involved Shootings and Custodial Death Letters under the Reports pull-down menu.

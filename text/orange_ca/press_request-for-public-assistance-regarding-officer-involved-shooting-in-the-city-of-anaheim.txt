REQUEST FOR PUBLIC ASSISTANCE
SANTA ANA – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) is seeking the public’s help identifying anyone with information regarding an officer-involved shooting in the City of Anaheim on July 21, 2012.
Anyone who witnessed the incident, is in possession of cell phone videos, other video recordings, or still photographs, or has additional information is asked to leave a message on the District Attorney Special Investigations hotline at
(714) 347-8544.
To protect the integrity of the investigation, the OCDA will not release any information regarding the investigation until it is completed. The media and public will be notified at that time and the investigative findings and legal conclusions will be available at
www.orangecountyda.com
.

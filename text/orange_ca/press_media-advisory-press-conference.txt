MEDIA ADVISORY – Press Conference
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the press conference
WHO:
Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Todd Spitzer and Senior Assistant District Attorney Ebrahim Baytieh
WHAT:
Will hold a press conference to present the investigation findings, video evidence, and legal conclusions regarding two fatal officer-involved shootings. The full reports, “OCDA Report Officer-Involved Shooting Investigation – Donadony Taylor” and “OCDA Report Officer-Involved Shooting Investigation – Katherine Brazeau,” will be made available after the press conference.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Feb. 28, 2019, at 10:00 a.m.
Doors will open at 9:30 a.m. for credentialed members of the press, OCDA staff, and invited guests.
WHERE:
First floor Law Library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701.

For Immediate Release

October 25, 2012
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney’s Office
WHAT: Will argue before the Appellate Division of the Superior Court to defend the County statute creating child safety zones by prohibiting registered sex offenders from entering County parks. This is the first case of its kind. The appellant is registered sex offender Hugo Godinez.
WHEN: Today, Thursday, Oct. 25, 2012, at 9:30 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-1, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
To read the details of the Hugo Godinez case, visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select from the homepage under Press Releases/Media Advisories the Nov. 17, 2011, press release titled, Registered Sex Offender Sentenced for Violating Orange County Child Safety Zone Ordinance by Entering Mile Square Regional Park on Cinco de Mayo.”

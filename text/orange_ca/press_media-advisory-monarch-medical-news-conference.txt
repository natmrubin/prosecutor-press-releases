MEDIA ADVISORY – Monarch Medical news conference
Date: April 20, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and California Insurance Commissioner Dave Jones
WHAT: Will hold a news conference today to announce charges filed against 21 doctors, two pharmacists, two business owners, and a physician assistant for a $40 million conspiracy to commit medical insurance fraud
WHEN:
Today, Thursday, April 20, 2017, at 10:30 a.m.
Doors will open at 10:00 a.m. and embargoed copies of the news release, remarks, and visuals will be made available for the press.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Watch remotely and ask questions through Facebook Live at
https://www.facebook.com/OCDATony
Senior Deputy District Attorney Shaddi Kamiabipour of the Insurance Fraud Unit is prosecuting these cases.

MEDIA ADVISORY – ISAIAS ARROYO SUPREME COURT OPINION
Case # 12ZF0158
Date: January 15, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Supreme Court of California
WHAT: Issued an opinion in People v. Isaias Arroyo that agreed with the Orange County District Attorney’s (OCDA) position to use the grand jury to indict juveniles eligible for adult prosecution.
Senior Deputy District Attorney Stephan Sauer of the Appellate & Training Unit argued the OCDA’s position in this matter. Senior Deputy District Attorney Mark Geller is prosecuting this case.
WHEN: Yesterday, Thursday, Jan. 14, 2016
WHERE: To read the court’s opinion, please visit the OCDA website, www.orangecountyda.org, and select “Supreme Court of California opinion in People v. Isaias Arroyo,” in Reports under the Reports pull-down menu.

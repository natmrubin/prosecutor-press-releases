MAN CHARGED WITH MURDER OF 70-YEAR-OLD WOMAN IN ANAHEIM
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was charged today with the murder of a 70-year-old woman in Anaheim.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Michael Carl Hallgren, 64, Anaheim
Charged on Nov. 26, 2018, with the following:
One felony count of murder
Sentencing Enhancements:
Personal use of a knife
Prior conviction for robbery in Los Angeles in 2001
51 years to life in state prison
Continued arraignment
Dec. 14, 2018, at 10:00 a.m.
Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
On Nov. 20, 2018, Hallgren is accused of attacking 70-year-old Lucinda Palma inside a residence in Anaheim.
Anaheim Police Department (APD), who investigated this case, responded to the residence and found the victim deceased.
APD arrested the defendant at the scene.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Janine Madera, Homicide Unit

MEDIA ADVISORY – WILLIAM BRADSHAW SENTENCING
For Immediate Release
February 22, 2007
Contact:
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
* The victims will have the opportunity to present victim impact statements
WHO: William Todd Bradshaw, 38
WHAT: Will be sentenced for a hit and run that permanently critically injured two mothers who were jogging in Dana Point.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, February 23, 2007 at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department W-18, West Justice Center, Westminster
For additional information on this case, please visit the Orange County District Attorney’s website at
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the press release “Homeless Driver Sentenced for Hitting and Permanently Injuring Two Mothers Jogging in Dana Point” from February 7, 2007.

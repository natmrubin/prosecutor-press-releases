Media Advisory – News Conference announcing Samuel Woodward case update
Case # 18HF0073
Date: August 1, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference
WHO:   Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and Senator Janet Nguyen of California’s 34
th
District
WHAT: Will hold a news conference announcing an update in the case against Samuel Woodward, who is accused of murdering 19-year-old Blaze Bernstein
WHEN:
Thursday,
Aug. 2, 2018, at 12:00 p.m.
This event is not open to the general public. Doors will open at 11:30 a.m. for members of the media and OCDA staff, who should be prepared to show their credentials.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701
Watch remotely through Facebook Live at
https://facebook.com/OCDATony

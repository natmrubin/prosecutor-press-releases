MEDIA ADVISORY – NUZZIO BEGAREN, GUILLERMO ESPINOZA INDICTMENT
For Immediate Release
Case # 12ZF0139
June 1, 2012
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Nuzzio Begaren, 51, Rancho Cucamonga, and Guillermo Espinoza, 37, Norwalk
WHAT: Begaren will be arraigned today on an indictment by the Orange County Grand Jury for the cold case conspiracy to murder his wife in 1998 to collect $1 million in life insurance. Espinoza was also indicted yesterday and remains a fugitive. Both are charged with one felony count each of special circumstances murder and conspiracy to commit murder with sentencing enhancements for murder for financial gain and murder in the commission of a robbery. If convicted, both defendants face a minimum sentence of life in state prison without the possibility of parole.
WHEN: Today, June, 1, 2012, at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-5, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
For additional information on this case, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the April 21, 2011, press release titled, “
Law Enforcement Seeks Public’s Help Apprehending Fourth Defendant Charged with 1998 Cold Case Conspiracy Murder of Wife to Collect $1 Million Life Insurance Policy
,” from Press Releases/Media Advisories.

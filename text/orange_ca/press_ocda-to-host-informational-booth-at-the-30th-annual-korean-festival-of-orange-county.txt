OCDA TO HOST INFORMATIONAL BOOTH AT THE 30TH ANNUAL KOREAN FESTIVAL OF ORANGE COUNTY
SANTA ANA –
The Orange County District Attorney’s (OCDA) Office is proud to announce its participation in the 30
th
Annual Korean Festival of Orange County tomorrow, Friday, Oct. 11, 2013, starting at 10:00 a.m. This weekend festival will be hosted in Buena Park in the McCumber Shopping Center off of Beach Boulevard and Malvern Avenue. The festival will include Korean food, cultural performances, and promotional booths.
In order to encourage community participation in law enforcement and community awareness regarding public safety, the OCDA Government and Community Relations Unit will host booth 104 and will be speaking with visitors and handing out information in English and Korean. The resource materials will cover information regarding victim resources, crime prevention, the criminal justice system, domestic violence, fraud, identity theft, as well as information on the OCDA in the biennial report.

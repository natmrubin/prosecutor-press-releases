March 20, 2013

Office: 714-347-8408

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will hold a media round-table to discuss the investigation and legal findings regarding the July 2012 officer-involved shooting of Manuel Diaz.
WHEN: Today, Wednesday, March 20, 2013, at 3:00 p.m.
WHERE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office law library, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
PLEASE NOTE:
Only credentialed media will be admitted. Media interested in attending must RSVP in writing to
Farrah.Emami@da.ocgov.com
by no later than 2:30 p.m.

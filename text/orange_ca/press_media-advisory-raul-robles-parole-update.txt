For Immediate Release
May 24, 2011
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: The Board of Parole Hearings, California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitations (Board)
WHAT: Despite opposition by the Orange County District Attorney’s Office, the Board determined a criminal street gang member, Raul Puente Robles, 33, formerly of Santa Ana, is suitable for parole. The inmate was convicted and sentenced to life in state prison in 1995 for the attempted shooting-murder of a rival one year earlier.
WHEN: Yesterday, May 23, 2011, at 3:00 p.m.
WHERE: Chuckawalla Valley State Prison, 19025 Wiley’s Well Road, Blythe, CA
*For further information, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
under Press Releases/Media Advisories and reference the May 20, 2011, Orange County District Attorney press release titled, “OCDA to Oppose Parole for Gang Member Sentenced to Life in Prison for 1994 Attempted Murder of Rival.”

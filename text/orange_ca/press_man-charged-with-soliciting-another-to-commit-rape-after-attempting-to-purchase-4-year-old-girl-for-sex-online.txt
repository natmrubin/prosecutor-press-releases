*The investigation in this case is ongoing
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was charged today with soliciting another to commit rape after attempting to purchase a 4-year-old girl for sex through an online escort.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Nicolas Ryan Castillo, 29, Yorba Linda
Charged on April 2, 2018, with the following felony count:
Soliciting another to commit lewd acts upon a child
Four years in state prison
Bail review hearing
April 6, 2018, 8:30 a.m.
Department C-55, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
In February 2018, Castillo is accused of contacting Jane Doe online and arranging to engage in commercial sex.
During the course of their communication, the defendant is accused of asking Jane Doe if she could procure a young child for him to meet with multiple times per month for a set fee, and describing in detail what sex acts he desired during those sessions.
Jane Doe contacted the Santa Ana Police Department (SAPD) to report Castillo’s statements.
SAPD detectives investigated this case and arrested Castillo on March 28, 2018.
The investigation in this case is ongoing. Anyone with additional information or who believes they have been a victim is encouraged to contact SAPD Detective Nick Vega at 714-245-8368 or Supervising District Attorney Investigator Frank Reynoso at 714-347-8560.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Daniel Varon, HEAT Unit

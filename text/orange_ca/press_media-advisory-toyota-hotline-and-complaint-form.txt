For Immediate Release
March 15, 2010
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
A hotline and complaint form are available through the Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) regarding the lawsuit against Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A., Inc.
Citizens and consumers wishing to leave a comment can
call the OCDA at (714) 347-0515
.
A “Toyota Sudden Acceleration Form” is available for consumers to report defects in their Toyota and Lexus brand vehicles on the home page of the OCDA website at
www.orangecountyda.com
.
For additional information on the civil lawsuit against Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A., Inc., please see
www.orangecountyda.com
and select under Press Releases/Media Advisories the March 12, 2010, press release titled “OCDA Files Consumer Protection Lawsuit Against Toyota for Endangering the Public by Knowingly Selling Defective Vehicles and Intentionally Hiding Defects from Consumers.”
A full copy of the civil complaint filed against Toyota is available on the homepage under “Media Center” at
www.orangecountyda.com
.

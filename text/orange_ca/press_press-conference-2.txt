PRESS CONFERENCE
For Immediate Release
October 12th, 2005
Contact:
Mark Macaulay
Media Relations Spokesperson
(714) 347-8405
Who
:     Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas.
What:
District Attorney to unveil Orange County’s Ten Most Wanted list.  He will also
introduce a new upgraded website containing helpful legal tips and resources.
The Ten Most Wanted list will enlist the assistance of the public in finding some
of the worst criminal offenders who have eluded capture.  The list will be
updated regularly with information about these and other cases.
When
:   11:15 a.m. on Thursday, October 13, 2005.
Where:
Orange County District Attorney’s Office, First Floor Library,
401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701.

MEDIA ADVISORY – NEWS CONFERENCE CANCELED
Date: October 2, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Has canceled the news conference scheduled for today to present the investigation findings, video evidence, and legal conclusions regarding the Orange Police Department fatal officer-involved shooting of Michael Anthony Perez.
The news conference will be rescheduled and the new date is to be determined.
“The Orange County District Attorney’s Office is united in our support for the people of Las Vegas, and all Americans, since this was an evil perpetrated on all of us. Our hearts go out to the victims and their families.”
WHEN: Today, Oct. 2, 2017

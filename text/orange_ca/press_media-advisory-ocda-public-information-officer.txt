WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Todd Spitzer
WHAT: Announced the appointment of Public Information Officer Kimberly Edds. All media inquiries should be directed to
Media@da.ocgov.com
or 714-347-8405. Edds can be reached at 714-504-1917 with urgent or after hours inquiries.
To request a speaker at an event or community meeting, please fill out the form on the OCDA’s website:

News Conference – Edwin Fuentes officer-involved shooting investigation
Date: June 19, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and Senior Assistant District Attorney Ebrahim Baytieh
WHAT: Will hold a news conference to present the investigation findings, video evidence, and legal conclusions regarding the Tustin Police Department fatal officer-involved shooting of Edwin Moreno Fuentes.
WHEN:
Tomorrow, June 20, 2018, at 10:00 a.m.
Doors will open at 9:30 a.m. for credentialed members of the press, OCDA staff, and invited guests. The full report, “OCDA Report Officer-Involved Shooting Investigation – Edwin Fuentes,” will also be made available.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701
Watch remotely through Facebook Live at
https://facebook.com/OCDATony

Date: January 8, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will be sworn in for his fifth term by U.S. District Judge David Carter. District Attorney Rackauckas will take the Oath of Office and deliver the State of Public Safety in Orange County address.
WHEN: Tuesday, Jan. 20, 2015, at 5:00 p.m.
WHERE: Orange County Hall of Administration, 333 West Santa Ana Boulevard, Santa Ana

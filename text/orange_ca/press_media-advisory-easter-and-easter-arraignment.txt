MEDIA ADVISORY – EASTER AND EASTER ARRAIGNMENT
For Immediate Release
Case # 12CF1831
July 13, 2012
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
Kent Wycliffe Easter, 38, and Jill Bjorkholm Easter, 39, both of Irvine
WHAT: At the request of the defense, the arraignment for the defendants will be advanced to Monday, July 16, 2012, from the previously scheduled July 17, 2012, date. The Easters are
charged with conspiring to have an elementary school parent volunteer arrested and imprisoned by planting drugs in her car.
WHEN: Monday, July 16, 2012, at 8:30 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-57, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana.
To read additional information regarding this case, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the June 19, 2012, press release titled, “Husband and Wife Charged with Conspiracy to Have School Volunteer Falsely Imprisoned by Planting Drugs in Her Car,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

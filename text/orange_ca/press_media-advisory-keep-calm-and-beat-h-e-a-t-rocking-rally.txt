For Immediate Release
September 6, 2013
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney’s (OCDA) Office
WHAT: Hosting the “Keep Calm and Beat H.E.A.T. Rocking & Rally,” to fight against human exploitation and trafficking
as part of a new initiative
to go after perpetrators of human trafficking and exploitation with vigor, while taking a comprehensive approach to solve the problem and bring community stakeholders together.
WHEN: Monday, Sept. 9, 2013, from 5:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
WHERE: Twila Reid Park, 3100 W. Orange Ave., Anaheim
Attendees are encouraged to wear red and bring their smart phones. There will be food trucks, free frozen yogurt, and a free t-shirt. There will be a live concert featuring Stan Frazier and Murphy Karges (Sugar Ray), Lewis Richards (Producer for Sublime and Dirty Heads), Scott Foster (solo-musician and star of Discovery Channel’s
Bering Sea Gold)
, and OCDA Investigator Damon Tucker (Parkaimoon).
For more information on the rally, please visit our website at
www.orangecountyda.com
, and select Support Grows for “Keep Calm & Beat H.E.A.T. Rocking & Rally,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

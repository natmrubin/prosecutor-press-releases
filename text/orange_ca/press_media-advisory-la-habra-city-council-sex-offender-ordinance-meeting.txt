For Immediate Release
September 19, 2011
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408

Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Senior Assistant District Attorney Mary Anne McCauley from the Orange County District Attorney’s Office
WHAT: Will attend the La Habra City Council meeting in support of enacting a City Ordinance to create a child safety zone in city parks to further protect children from registered sex offenders. The Council will discuss whether La Habra should enact a City Ordinance similar to the County Ordinance proposed by Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Supervisor Shawn Nelson (Fourth District) and passed unanimously by the Orange County Board of Supervisors on April 5, 2011. The County Ordinance makes it a misdemeanor for registered sex offenders to enter County recreational areas where children regularly gather.
WHEN: Today, Monday, Sept. 19, 2011, at 6:30 p.m.
WHERE: City Hall, 201 E. La Habra Boulevard, La Habra
To read the full County Ordinance, please select the April 5, 2011, press release titled “Orange County Board of Supervisors Unanimously Votes to Adopt New Law to Keep Registered Sex Offenders Out of Parks, Harbors, Beaches and Playgrounds,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories at
www.orangecountyda.com

MEDIA ADVISORY- PREMIUM INSURANCE FRAUD PRESS CONFERENCE
For Immediate Release
April 29, 2009
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Video of the arrests and evidence seized will be available at the press conference
WHO: District Attorney Tony Rackauckas, Insurance Commissioner Steve Poizner, and representatives from the Employment Development Department, and the Franchise Tax Board.
WHAT: Will discuss the arrest of and charges against husband and wife defendants in the largest known Workers’ Compensation Insurance fraud case in California’s history totaling $38 million.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Thursday, April 30, 2009, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana 92701
###
AVISO DE PRENSA
*Video de los arrestos y evidencia incautada será disponible en la conferencia de prensa
QUIEN: El Fiscal Tony Rackauckas, el Comisario del Departamento de Seguros Steve Poizner, y representantes del Departamento del Desarrollo del Empleo, y el Franchise Tax Board.
QUE: Hablaran sobre el arresto y acusaciones contra un marido y su esposa en el caso más grande en la historia de California de fraude de Compensación de Trabajadores de $38 millones.
CUANDO: Mañana, jueves, 30 de abril, 2009, a las 10:00 de la mañana.
DONDE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana 92701

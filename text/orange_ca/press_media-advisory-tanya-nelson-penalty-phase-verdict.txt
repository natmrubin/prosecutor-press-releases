Case # 05WF1591
March 2, 2010
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408

Farrah Emami

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
Tanya Jaime Nelson, 45, Roanoke Rapids, NC
WHAT: A verdict will be read in the penalty phase of the homicide case against Nelson. She was convicted Feb. 16, 2010, of murdering an Orange County fortune teller and her daughter with the intention of robbing the victim, including stealing her expensive jewelry.
WHEN:
Immediately
, today, Tuesday, March 2, 2010
WHERE: Department C-35, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

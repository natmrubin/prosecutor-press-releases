MEDIA ADVISORY – CHARLES LEE CLARK ARRAIGNMENT
For Immediate Release
Case # 13CF0177
January 24, 2013
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Charles Lee Clark, 74, Beavercreek, OR
WHAT: Was extradited back to Orange County Jan. 23, 2013, and will be arraigned tomorrow on one felony count of murder with a sentencing enhancement for the personal use of a deadly weapon for the 1990 cold case stabbing-murder of his roommate. Clark also has two previous Strike convictions for a robbery in 1975 and 1990. If convicted on all counts, he faces a maximum sentence of life in state prison.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, Jan. 25, 2013, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
For additional information on this case, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the Jan. 23, 2013, press release titled, “Third-Striker Extradited to Orange County for 1990 Tustin Cold Case Stabbing-Murder of Roommate,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

Media Advisory – OCDA report regarding Santa Ana Mayor Miguel Pulido
Date: May 22, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: The OCDA has published the report and investigative findings of the joint investigation conducted by the OCDA and California Fair Political Practices Commission regarding Santa Ana Mayor Miguel Pulido. The full report, “Allegations of Criminal Conduct and Conflicts of Interest by Mayor Miguel Pulido,” is available at www.orangecountyda.org by selecting Reports and Settlements under the Reports pull-down menu.
WHEN: Today, May 22, 2015

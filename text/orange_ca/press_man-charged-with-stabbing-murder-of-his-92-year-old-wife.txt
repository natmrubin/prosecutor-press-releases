Date: November 5, 2018
MAN CHARGED WITH STABBING MURDER OF HIS 92-YEAR-OLD WIFE
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was charged today with the stabbing murder of his 92-year-old wife.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
William Frederick Dusablon, 92, Fullerton
Charged today with the following:
One felony count of murder
Sentencing Enhancement:
Personal use of a deadly weapon
26 years to life in state prison
Continued Arraignment
Nov. 14, 2018, at 8:30 a.m.
Department C-5, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
On Nov. 4, 2018, Dusablon is accused of stabbing his wife, 92-year-old Chiyoko Dusablon.
At approximately 6:30 a.m., the Fullerton Police Department (FPD) responded to the defendant’s home.
The victim was pronounced dead at the scene.
FPD investigated this case and arrested Dusablon at his home.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Jennifer Walker, Homicide Unit

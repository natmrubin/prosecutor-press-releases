MEDIA ADVISORY – HOSPITALITY TRAINING EVENT
Date: November 11, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Media are invited to attend and the elected officials will be available for interviews
WHO: United States Congressman Ed Royce, Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas, members of the Orange County Human Trafficking Task Force, including Anaheim Police Deputy Chief Julian Harvey, and law enforcement officers.
WHAT: Will host an anti-human trafficking training seminar designed especially for the hospitality industry to identify the signs of human trafficking and how to report it. Guest speakers include a victim survivor and a representative from the FBI, who specializes in human trafficking cases.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Thursday, Nov. 12, 2015
Registration at 8:00 a.m.
Training from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
WHERE: California State University, Fullerton, 800 North State College Boulevard, Titan Student Union

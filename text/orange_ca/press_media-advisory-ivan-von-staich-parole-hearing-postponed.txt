MEDIA ADVISORY – IVAN VON STAICH PAROLE HEARING POSTPONED
For Immediate Release
November 29, 2012
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: The Board of Parole Hearings, California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitations (Board)
WHAT: The Board has postponed the parole hearing to an undetermined time for inmate Ivan Von Staich, pending additional review. The 55-year-old inmate was found guilty of the 1983 attempted murder of his ex-girlfriend and murder of her husband, and was sentenced May 23, 1986, to 30 years to life in prison.
WHEN: Yesterday, November 28, 2012
WHERE: California Men’s Colony, San Luis Obispo, CA
For further information, please select the Nov. 27, 2012, press release titled, “OCDA to Oppose Parole of Inmate Convicted of Attempted Murder of Ex-Girlfriend and Shooting-Murder of Her Husband,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories at
www.orangecountyda.com
.

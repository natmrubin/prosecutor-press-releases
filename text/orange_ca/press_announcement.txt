ANNOUNCEMENT
For Immediate Release
November 23rd, 2005
Contact:
Susan Kang Schroeder
Deputy District Attorney
(714) 347-8408 (Office)
(714) 292-2718 (Cell)
SANTA ANA
— Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas announced today that spokesperson Mark Macaulay has accepted a new job and will be leaving his position in media relations.
“
Mark worked tirelessly in serving Orange County.
He was an integral part of our law enforcement team and we appreciate his contributions
,”
said Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas.
All media inquiries should be directed to Deputy District Attorney Susan Kang Schroeder.
A search for a new spokesperson will begin immediately.
Anyone interested in applying for the position should contact Human Resources Manager Claudette LeBlue at 714-347-8440 or go to the website at
www.orangecountyda.com
.

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
John Fitzgerald Kennedy, 43, Long Beach
WHAT: The verdict will be read in the guilt phase of the Kennedy trial. He
is charged with felonies for two counts of special circumstances murder in the murder-for-profit killings of Thomas and Jackie Hawks on their yacht off Newport Beach in 2004. He has a prior strike for a 1988 attempted murder conviction.
The District Attorney is seeking the death penalty in this case.
WHEN: Today at 1:30 p.m., Thursday, Feb. 19, 2009
WHERE: Department C-35, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney’s Chief of Staff Susan Kang Schroeder
WHAT: Will attend the Huntington Beach City Council meeting in support of enacting a City Ordinance to create a child safety zone in city parks to further protect children from registered sex offenders. The Council will discuss whether Huntington Beach should enact a City Ordinance similar to the County Ordinance proposed by Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Supervisor Shawn Nelson (Fourth District) and passed unanimously by the Orange County Board of Supervisors on April 5, 2011. The County Ordinance makes it a misdemeanor for registered sex offenders to enter County recreational areas where children regularly gather.
WHEN: Monday, Nov. 7, 2011, at 7:00 p.m.
WHERE: City Hall, 2000 Main Street, Huntington Beach
To read the full County Ordinance, please select the April 5, 2011, press release titled “Orange County Board of Supervisors Unanimously Votes to Adopt New Law to Keep Registered Sex Offenders Out of Parks, Harbors, Beaches and Playgrounds,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories at
www.orangecountyda.com
.
To read the agenda item, please select “Agendas & Minutes,” under the Government tab-down bar on the homepage
https://www.huntingtonbeachca.gov/
. Select “City Council Agenda,” click on the agenda for Nov. 7, 2011.

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHAT: Two fugitives, Gabriel Melendez, 37, and Daniel Rojas, 27, are now featured on the Orange County District Attorney’s Most Wanted list. Melendez has been a fugitive for 18 years and is wanted for murder. Rojas failed to appear in court for jury trial on attempted murder charges and has been a fugitive for three years.
WHERE: To view the profiles, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and click on Most Wanted on the homepage.

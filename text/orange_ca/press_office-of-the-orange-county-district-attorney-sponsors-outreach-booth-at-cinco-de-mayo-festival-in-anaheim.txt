OFFICE OF THE ORANGE COUNTY DISTRICT ATTORNEY SPONSORS OUTREACH BOOTH AT CINCO DE MAYO FESTIVAL IN ANAHEIM
Santa Ana – The Orange County District Attorney’s Government and Community Relations Unit will host an outreach booth at the annual Cinco de Mayo Festival this Saturday and Sunday (May 1 and 2), at La Palma Park located at the corner of Harbor Boulevard and La Palma in Anaheim.
Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas, OCDA staff, and volunteers will be available to answer questions from the public and to hand out bilingual pamphlets and resource booklets about the criminal justice system and the role of the District Attorney’s Office in the Hispanic community of Orange County.  In addition to the informational materials, the OCDA booth will also feature prize drawings, free face painting, arts and crafts, and balloons.
The Orange County District Attorney’s Government and Community Relations booth will be open from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.

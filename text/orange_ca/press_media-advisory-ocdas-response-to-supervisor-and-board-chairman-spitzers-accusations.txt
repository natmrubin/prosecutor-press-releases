MEDIA ADVISORY

WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Sent a letter to Orange County Board of Supervisors Chairman Todd Spitzer in response to unfounded and uninformed accusations made at the last Board of Supervisors meeting on Jan. 27, 2015.
WHEN: Today, Monday, Feb. 2, 2015
WHERE: To read the letter, please visit
www.orangecountyda.org

Media Advisory: Ford and OC GRIP incentive
Date: December 3, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Ford Motor Company (Ford), Taco Bell, RMD Group, OC GRIP and its partners including Orange County Sheriff’s Department, Garden Grove Police Department, Fullerton Police Department, Santa Ana Police Department, Santa Ana Unified School District Police Department, Buena Park Police Department, Orange County Probation Department, and the Orange Police Department
WHAT: Will reward 120 local elementary and middle school at-risk students with a visit to Ford’s Irvine design facilities tomorrow, as an incentive for positive behavior, perfect attendance, and staying out of gangs. To earn this reward, the students met a challenge to have no unexcused absences, criminal activity or arrests, violence or weapons on campus, and gang-related clothing, writing, or behavior.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, Dec. 4, 2015, at 8:30 a.m.
WHERE: Ford Regional Headquarters, 3 Glen Bell Way, #110, Irvine, CA 92618
RSVP: Media interested in attending are asked to arrive at the Ford building by 8:30 a.m. tomorrow, and may RSVP via phone or email to Orange County District Attorney Spokesperson Roxi Fyad at
Roxi.Fyad@da.ocgov.com
, (714) 347-8405, Ford Communications Manager Debra Hotaling at
dhotali1@ford.com
, (949)235-5844, or Jaymie Robinson at
jaymie.robinson@ogilvy.com
, (310) 383-7269

MEDIA ADVISORY – Press Conference
Date: October 12, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the press conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will hold a press conference today to discuss charges against two sister companies for their role in the unlawful sale of fetal tissue for valuable consideration.
WHEN:
Today, Wednesday, Oct. 12, 2016, at 10:00 a.m.
Doors will open at 9:00 a.m. and embargoed copies of the complaint and visuals will be made available.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana

YOUTH MINISTRY MENTOR CONVICTED OF COMMITTING LEWD ACTS ON TWO JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL BOYS
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A youth ministry mentor was convicted yesterday of committing lewd acts on two junior high school boys.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Ruven Meulenberg, 33, Lake Forest
Found guilty by a jury on Aug. 22, 2018, of the following felony counts:
(3) Lewd acts on a child
Lewd acts on a child under age 14
12 years and six months in state prison
Sentencing
Oct. 5, 2018, 8:30 a.m.
Department C-38, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
At the time of the crime, Meulenberg was a volunteer youth mentor at Saddleback Church in Lake Forest in a position of trust with access to children.
Between May 2016 and May 2017, Meulenberg committed lewd acts on 13-year-old John Doe 1.
On May 22, 2017, the defendant sexually molested John Doe 1 and John Doe 2, who were then 14 years old.
On May 24, 2017, a Saddleback Church representative reported suspected inappropriate conduct to the Orange County Sheriff’s Department (OCSD), who investigated this case.
Meulenberg was arrested by OCSD on Thursday, May 25, 2017.
Prosecutor: Deputy District Attorney Courtney Thom, Sexual Assault Unit

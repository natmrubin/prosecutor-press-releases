MEDIA ADVISORY – UNITY OUTPATIENT SURGERY CENTER PRESS CONFERENCE
For Immediate Release
May 16, 2007
Contact:
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405

Jennifer Kerns

California Department of Insurance
Office: 916-492-3566

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: California Insurance Commissioner Steve Poizner, Deputy Director of the Medical Board of California Kimberly Kirchmeyer, a representative of the Franchise Tax Board, and Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas.
WHAT: Will hold a press conference on the arrest of three medical doctors involved in the Unity Outpatient Surgery Center scheme; doctors accused of performing over 1,000 unnecessary surgical procedures and fraudulently billing over $30,500,000
dollars from medical insurance companies.
WHEN:
This morning
, Wednesday, May 16, 2007 at 10:30 a.m.
WHERE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office, Law Library,
401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, CA 92701.

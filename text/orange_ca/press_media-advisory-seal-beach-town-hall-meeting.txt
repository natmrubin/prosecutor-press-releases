For Immediate Release

Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408

Farrah Emami
Spokesperson

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Senior Deputy District Attorney Scott Simmons
WHAT: Will attend the City of Seal Beach Town Hall meeting regarding defendant Scott Evans Dekraai, 42, Huntington Beach, who is charged with eight felony counts of special circumstances murder for committing multiple murders and one felony count of attempted murder. On Oct. 12, 2011, Dekraai is accused of committing the largest mass-murder in Orange County history by shooting innocent victims inside and outside the Seal Beach Salon Meritage.
WHEN: Today, Thursday, Oct. 27, 2011, at 7:00 p.m.
WHERE: McGaugh Elementary School Auditorium, 1698 Bolsa Avenue, Seal Beach
To read the full press release on this case, visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the Oct. 14, 2011, press release titled, “District Attorney to Seek Death Penalty Against Seal Beach Salon Shooter for Largest Mass Murder in Orange County History,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.
To view the full video of the press conference held by District Attorney Rackauckas and Seal Beach Acting Chief of Police Tim Olson, visit the Media Center on the homepage at
www.orangecountyda.com
.  Internet Explorer will run the video directly through Windows Media. Certain Internet browsers, such as Firefox, Chrome, and Safari may require the video to be downloaded.

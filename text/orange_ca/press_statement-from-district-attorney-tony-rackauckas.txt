STATEMENT FROM DISTRICT ATTORNEY TONY RACKAUCKAS
“California lost a great crime fighter with the passing of former California Attorney General and Governor George Deukmejian. He was a staunch advocate for public safety including sponsoring the successful “use a gun, go to prison” bill. We worked together to oust three rogue California Supreme Court Justices who refused to uphold the rule of law.”

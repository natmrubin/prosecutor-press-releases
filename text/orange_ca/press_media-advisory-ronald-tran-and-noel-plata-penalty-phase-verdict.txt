MEDIA ADVISORY – RONALD TRAN AND NOEL PLATA PENALTY PHASE VERDICT
For Immediate Release
Case # 01HF0193
November 5, 2007
Contact:
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
Ronald Tri Tran, 32, and Noel Jesse Plata, 32, both of
Santa Ana
WHAT: The verdict of the penalty phase will be read for the death penalty case against Tran and Plata, who were convicted October 23, 2007 of the torture and murder of an 18-year-old Irvine girl.
WHEN: Today, Monday, November 5, 2007 at 1:30 p.m.
WHERE: Department C-40, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

Media Advisory – 2015 Hospitality Anti-Human Trafficking Training Seminar
Date: November 13, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: United States Congressman Ed Royce, Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas, members of the Orange County Human Trafficking Task Force, including Anaheim Police Deputy Chief Julian Harvey, and law enforcement officers.
WHAT: Hosted an anti-human trafficking training seminar at California State University, Fullerton, designed especially for the hospitality industry to identify the signs of human trafficking and how to report it. Guest speakers included a victim survivor and a representative from the FBI, who specializes in human trafficking cases.
WHERE: To read OCDA Tony Rackauckas’ remarks from the seminar, visit the following link:
OCDA 2015 Hospitality Anti-Human Trafficking Remarks
To watch an edited video of today’s training seminar visit the link below. (Portions of the seminar have been edited to protect the identity of undercover law enforcement officers):
https://youtu.be/mAyjPbJ8Rt0
To view information about how to look for warning signs of human trafficking within the hospitality industry, visit
www.orangecountyda.org
and select Human Trafficking Information under the Media Center pull-down menu (copies in Spanish are also available upon request).
WHEN: Yesterday, Nov. 12, 2015

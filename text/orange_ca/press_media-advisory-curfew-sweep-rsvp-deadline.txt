For Immediate Release
November 12, 2010
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405

MEDIA ADVISORY
*Reminder: Today is the last day for media to RSVP
WHO: The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) and 18 law enforcement agencies including the Orange County Sheriff’s Department, Department of Probation, the police departments from the Cities of Anaheim, Brea, Buena Park, Costa Mesa, Fullerton, Garden Grove, La Habra, La Palma, Los Alamitos, Newport Beach, Orange, Placentia, Santa Ana, and Tustin, the California Highway Patrol, and the Cal State Fullerton and Santa Ana Unified School Police.
WHAT: Will conduct the largest-ever Orange County curfew sweeps aimed at identifying juveniles in public past curfew in an ongoing County-wide gang prevention effort.
MEDIA: Media who wish to attend should contact OCDA Spokesperson Farrah Emami at (714) 347-8405 no later than Friday, Nov. 12, 2010, for time, date, and location details.
Interviews will be available in almost every Orange County city (including unincorporated areas) and may be provided in English, Spanish, Korean, and Vietnamese with advance notice.

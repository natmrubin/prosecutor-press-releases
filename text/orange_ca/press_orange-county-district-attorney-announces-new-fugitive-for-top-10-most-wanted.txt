ORANGE COUNTY DISTRICT ATTORNEY ANNOUNCES NEW FUGITIVE FOR TOP 10 MOST WANTED
SANTA ANA
– District Attorney Tony Rackauckas announced today a new fugitive for Orange County District Attorney’s Top Ten Most Wanted.
Taylor Winston Wright, 37, is wanted for charges surrounding manufacturing PCP in a quiet South County suburb.
He was last seen June 4, 2005 in Ladera Ranch, running from law enforcement after they discovered a dangerous drug lab in his apartment and garage.
An arrest warrant #
02901747
was issued for $500,000 on June 7, 2005.

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Manuel Anthony Ramos, 38, and Jay Patrick Cicinelli, 40
WHAT: Preliminary hearing in the case involving the beating-death of 37-year-old Kelly Thomas on July 5, 2011, by Fullerton Police Department Officers Ramos and Cicinelli.
WHEN: Today, Monday, May 7, 2012, at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-1, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
To read the full press release on this case, visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the Sept. 21, 2011, press release titled, “OCDA Charges Two Fullerton Police Officers for the Beating-Death of 37-Year-Old Homeless Man.”

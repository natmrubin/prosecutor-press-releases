MEDIA ADVISORY – Ezequiel Barragan arraignment
Case # 15HF0455
Date: May 21, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Ezequiel Barragan, 48, Aliso Viejo
WHAT: Will be in court tomorrow for a continued arraignment. A press release in Spanish was released today looking for potential additional victims in the Latino community because the defendant also coached at Santa Ana High School and travelled extensively between Orange County and Mexico.
You can read the press release in English or in Spanish in the links below.
OCDA Seeks Public’s Help Identifying Potential Additional Victims of High School Teacher Charged with Sexually Assaulting Boy Under 14 Years Old and Possessing Child Pornography
El Fiscal del Condado de Orange Está Pidiendo la Ayuda Del Público en Identificar Más Víctimas Latinas de un Profesor de la Secundaria y Entrenador de Béisbol Juvenil Acusado de Abusar Sexualmente a un Niño Menor de 14 y Por Tener en su Posesión Pornografía Infantil
WHEN: Tomorrow, May 22, 2015, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana

MAN CHARGED WITH SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES MURDER OF FATHER WHILE WALKING ON TRAIL NEAR THEIR LAGUNA NIGUEL HOME
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was charged with the special circumstances murder of his father while they walked on a trail near their Laguna Niguel home.
Defendant
Charges
Minimum Sentence
Court Date
Maximilian Gregory Ludwig, 18, Laguna Niguel
Charged on Nov. 27, 2018, with the following:
One felony count of murder
Sentencing Enhancement
Special circumstances – lying in wait
Life in state prison without the possibility of parole
Arraignment
Nov. 27, 2018, time to be determined
Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case
On Nov. 23, 2018, at approximately 4:00 p.m., Maximilian Ludwig is accused of leading his father, 59-year-old Christopher Ludwig, on a walking trail near Highlands Avenue in Laguna Niguel.
The defendant is accused of attacking the victim with rocks and stomping on the victim’s head until Christopher Ludwig died as a result of blunt force trauma.
Maximilian Ludwig called 911 and the Orange County Sheriff’s Department (OCSD) arrested the defendant at the scene. OCSD investigated this case.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Troy Pino, Homicide Unit

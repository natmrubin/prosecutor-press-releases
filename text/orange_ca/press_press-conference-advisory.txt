PRESS CONFERENCE ADVISORY
For Immediate Release
May 23rd, 2005
Contact:
Mark Macaulay
Media Relations Spokesperson
(714) 347-8405
WHO:       George Joseph England, 60, aka Stephen Arthur Seagoe, who fled Orange County in 1977after being convicted of molesting two 10-year-old girls. Captured in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida on May 19, 2005, by the combined efforts of the OCDA, FBI, and other federal agencies.
WHAT:     Press Conference to discuss the details of the investigation and capture of
this convicted sexual predator.
WHEN:    TODAY, May 23, 2005, at 3 PM
WHERE: First Floor Library, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA 92701.

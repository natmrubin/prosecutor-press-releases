MEDIA ADVISORY
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE:  July 6, 2006
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will swear-in Susan Lee as a deputy district attorney.  She is the ninth Korean-American prosecutor hired by Mr. Rackauckas in the last seven years.
WHEN: Friday, July 7, 2006, 10:30 a.m.
WHERE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA  92701.

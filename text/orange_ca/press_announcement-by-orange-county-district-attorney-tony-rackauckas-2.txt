ANNOUNCEMENT BY ORANGE COUNTY DISTRICT ATTORNEY TONY RACKAUCKAS
SANTA ANA – Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas announced today the appointment of Susan Kang Schroeder as his Chief of Staff.
Schroeder has been serving as the District Attorney’s Public Affairs Counsel.
She has been a prosecutor at the Orange County District Attorney’s Office since 1999 and was a prosecutor for the City of Anaheim from 1995 to 1999.
She received Bachelor of Arts degrees in journalism and political science conferred by the University of Southern California and her Juris Doctorate from the University of San Diego. The Chief of Staff position will perform the same duties as Public Affairs Counsel.

MEDIA ADVISORY- JOHN HINES ARRAIGNMENT
For Immediate Release
Case # 11CF1085
May 3, 2011
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: John David Hines, 38, Huntington Beach
WHAT: Will be arraigned on charges of severely injuring a cyclist in a mid-day hit-and-run crash while driving with a blood alcohol level of .24 percent, three times the legal limit. The defendant, a Captain with the Long Beach Fire Department, turned himself in to the court this morning on a $250,000 warrant.
WHEN:
Immediately,
this morning,
Tuesday, May 3, 2011
WHERE: Department C-55, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
To read the complete press release on this case, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select
“Fire Department Captain Charged with Injuring Bicyclist in Drunken Driving Hit-And-Run Crash”
from April 27, 2011, under Press Releases/Media Advisories on the homepage.

MEDIA ADVISORY – NEWS CONFERENCE REGARDING MICHAEL PEREZ OFFICER-INVOLVED SHOOTING INVESTIGATION
Date: September 29, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and Assistant District Attorney Ebrahim Baytieh
WHAT: Will hold a news conference to present the investigation findings, video evidence, and legal conclusions regarding the Orange Police Department fatal officer-involved shooting of Michael Anthony Perez.
WHEN:
Monday, Oct. 2, 2017, at 11:00 a.m.
Doors will open at 10:30 a.m. for credentialed members of the press, OCDA staff, and invited guests. The full report, “OCDA Report Officer-Involved Shooting Investigation – Michael Perez.,” will also be made available.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Watch remotely through Facebook Live at
https://facebook.com/OCDATony

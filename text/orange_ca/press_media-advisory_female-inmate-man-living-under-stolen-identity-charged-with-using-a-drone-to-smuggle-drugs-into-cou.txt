MEDIA ADVISORY: Orange County District Attorney, Sheriff to Provide Critical Update on Search for Aliso Viejo Serial Rapist
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: September 20, 2021
MEDIA ADVISORY
Kimberly Edds
Public Information Officer
Office: 714-347-8405, Cell: 714-504-1917
Sgt. Todd Hylton
Public Information Officer
Orange County Sheriff’s Department
Office: 714-647-7042, Cell: 714-904-7042
media@da.ocgov.com
SANTA ANA, Calif. – Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer and Orange County Sheriff Don Barnes will hold a press conference at 12 p.m. today to provide a critical update on the search for a serial rapist in Aliso Viejo.
Since January 2020, at least three women have been snatched off running trails in an Aliso Viejo park and choked unconscious in an attempt to sexually assault the women. One of the women was raped.
Joint Press Conference
Monday, September 20, 2021
12 p.m.
Orange County District Attorney’s Office
First Floor Media Room
300 N. Flower Street
Santa Ana, CA
The press conference will be livestreamed on the Orange County District Attorney’s Facebook @OCDAToddSpitzer
Media parking is available on West 3rd Street or in the adjacent parking structure. Due to ongoing street construction in the area, the parking structure is only accessible via West 3rd Street and you may experience traffic delays as a result of construction.
Doors will open at 11:30 a.m. for members of the media.

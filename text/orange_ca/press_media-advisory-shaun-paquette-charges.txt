MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Shaun Bradley Paquette, 29, Ladera Ranch
WHAT: Was charged today with one felony count each of assault with a semiautomatic firearm and child abuse and endangerment, and one misdemeanor count of domestic violence battery. If convicted, he faces a maximum sentence of 11 years and four months in state prison.
WHEN: Arraignment tomorrow, Wednesday, July 2, 2014, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) is investigating the officer-involved shooting incident involving this defendant. It is the policy of the OCDA to not release further details.

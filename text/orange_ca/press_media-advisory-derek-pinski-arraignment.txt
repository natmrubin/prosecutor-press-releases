For Immediate Release
Case # 12CF2594
September 12, 2012
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Derek Henry Pinski, 24, Laguna Hills
WHAT: Was brought back today from Arizona to Orange County on charges of one felony count of murder with a sentencing enhancement for the personal use of a deadly weapon. It is
expected that Pinski will be arraigned tomorrow.
WHEN: Arraignment tomorrow, Thursday, Sept. 13, 2012, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
For additional information on this case, please visit the homepage at
www.orangecountyda.com
and select under Press Releases/Media Advisories the Sept. 4, 2012, press release titled, “Man Who Fled to Arizona Charged with Stabbing-Murder of Girlfriend in Laguna Hills.”

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Daniel Guerra, 20, of Dana Point.
WHAT: Set to be arraigned on two counts of raping an 81-year-old woman in her home in Dana Point.  He is also charged with first degree residential burglary, kidnapping to commit a sex offense, and a misdemeanor charge of injuring a utility line.  Deputy District Attorney Lynda Fernandez in the Sexual Assault unit has been assigned to prosecute the case.
WHEN:    8:30 AM, Friday April 8th, 2005
WHERE: Department S2, South Justice Center, 30143 Crown Valley Parkway, Laguna Niguel, CA 92677.

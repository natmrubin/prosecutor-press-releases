Media Advisory – Benjamin Golden plea
Case # 15WM14655
Date: November 17, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Benjamin Allen Golden, 33
WHAT: Pleaded guilty to one misdemeanor count of battery on a taxi driver. He was sentenced to 60 days in county jail, three years of informal probation, and ordered to pay restitution.
On Oct. 30, 2015, Golden repeatedly struck his Uber driver’s head after he was ordered out of the vehicle by the victim.
WHEN: Today, Nov. 17, 2016
Costa Mesa Police Department investigated this case.
Deputy District Attorney Scott Wooldridge of the West Justice Center prosecuted this case.

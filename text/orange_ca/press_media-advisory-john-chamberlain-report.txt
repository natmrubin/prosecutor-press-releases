For Immediate Release
April 4, 2008
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405

MEDIA ADVISORY
What: Press conference about the District Attorney’s Report on the findings of the Special Criminal Grand Jury investigation into John Chamberlain’s death.
When:
Monday, April 7, 2008, at 9:30 a.m.
·
At 9:30 a.m., the Orange County District Attorney will be pre-releasing an embargoed Report to the members of the media. The embargo will be lifted at the end of the press conference.
·
At 10:30 a.m., the Orange County District Attorney will present the Report.
Where: District Attorney’s Office,
401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, in the Law Library.

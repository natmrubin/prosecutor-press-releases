SANTA ANA, Calif. – A fugitive was arrested yesterday after failing to appear for a preliminary hearing in January 2018.

Circumstances of the Case
Between Jan. 3, 2014, and Oct. 31, 2016, Mendoza-Marin is accused of committing lewd acts on Jane Doe, who was 12 years old at the start of these crimes.
Jane Doe’s father reported these incidents to the Orange County Sheriff’s Department (OCSD), who investigated this case.
OCSD arrested the defendant on Nov. 23, 2016.
Mendoza-Marin posted $100,000 bail on Nov. 29, 2016, and failed to appear for his preliminary hearing on Jan. 12, 2018. A warrant was issued for his arrest.
On Oct. 30, 2018, the United States Marshal Service successfully served the warrant and arrested the defendant in Cerritos.

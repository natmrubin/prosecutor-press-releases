MEDIA ADVISORY
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: December 7, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and the Orange County Gang Reduction Intervention Partnership (“OC GRIP”)
WHAT: Will reward 45 local at-risk elementary school students with a visit to the Ocean Institute in Dana Point as an incentive for positive behavior, perfect attendance, and staying out of gangs. Students will participate in hands-on interactive learning experiences and have the opportunity to sail on two different vessels.
WHEN: Friday, Dec. 8, 2017, at 8:30 a.m.
WHERE: Ocean Institute, 24200 Dana Point Harbor Drive in Dana Point
Media interested in attending are asked to arrive at the Ocean Institute by 8:30 a.m. and may RSVP via phone to Orange County District Attorney Public Affairs Unit at: (714) 347-8405.

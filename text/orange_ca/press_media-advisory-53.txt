MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:   John Fitzgerald Kennedy, 39, of Long Beach.
WHAT: Set to be arraigned on charges of murder for taking part in the killing of Thomas and Jackie Hawks. The Hawks were living on their 55-foot yacht “Well Deserved” in Newport Beach when they went missing. They had recently advertised the boat for sale in several yacht magazines. Co-defendants Skylar Deleon, Alonso Machain, and Myron Gardner, Sr. will be arraigned on April 15, 2005 in H2 of the Harbor Justice Center in Newport Beach.
WHEN:    1:30 PM, Monday, March 14, 2005.
WHERE: Department H2, Harbor Justice Center, 4601 Jamboree Road, Newport Beach, CA 92660.

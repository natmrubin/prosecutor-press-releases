MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Sid Landau, 70
WHAT: The Orange County District Attorney’s Office will oppose a motion by serial child molester Landau, who has filed a petition to be released from his Sexually Violent Predator civil commitment at Coalinga State Hospital. The OCDA will argue that Landau’s petition should be denied because he is a continued threat to children and the community.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, Sept. 3, 2010, at 9 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-43, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

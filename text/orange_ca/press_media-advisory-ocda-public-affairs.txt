Media Advisory – OCDA Public Affairs
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Todd Spitzer
WHAT: Announced an update regarding the Public Affairs Unit, which looks forward to working with you on media and community requests.
All media inquiries should be directed to
Media@da.ocgov.com
or 714-347-8405.  To request a speaker at an event or community meeting, please fill out the form on the OCDA’s website:
https://ocdistrictattorney.gov/howdoi/request.asp
Until a Public Information Officer (PIO) is appointed, please call or text Melanie Eustice, Chief of Administration and Public Affairs, at 714-975-3002 with urgent or after hours inquiries. New contact information for the PIO will be distributed in the near future.

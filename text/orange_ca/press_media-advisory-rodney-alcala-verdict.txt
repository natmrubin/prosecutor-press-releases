MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Rodney James Alcala, 66
WHAT: A verdict will be read in the murder case against Alcala. The defendant has been is on trial for the murder of 12-year-old Robin Samsoe in Orange County and four Los Angeles County women, Jill Parenteau, Charlotte Lamb, Georgia Wixted, and Jill Barcomb.
WHEN: Today, Thursday, Feb. 25, 2010, at 1:30 p.m.
WHERE: Department C-45, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

MEDIA ADVISORY – PANDA PRESS CONFERENCE VIDEO
Date: April 8, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published video of today’s press conference announcing the newly launched PAWS Assist the Needs of the District Attorney (PANDA) program, where Pets Are Wonderful Support (PAWS) volunteers and their therapy dogs will comfort sexual assault victims as they meet with prosecutors.
To watch the video, please visit the following link:
https://youtu.be/n1rHogNcBFk

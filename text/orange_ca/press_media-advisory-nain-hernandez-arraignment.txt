Media Advisory – Nain Hernandez arraignment
Case # 16HF0002
Date: January 4, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
* No further information will be released at this time
WHO: Nain Issac Nieto Hernandez, 29, Santa Ana
WHAT: Has been charged with and will be arraigned on one felony count of murder. If convicted, Hernandez faces a maximum sentence of 25 years to life in state prison.
WHEN: Today, Jan. 4, 2016, at a time to be determined this afternoon
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
The investigation in this case is ongoing. No further information will be released at this time.
Senior Deputy District Attorney Matt Murphy of the Homicide Unit is prosecuting this case.

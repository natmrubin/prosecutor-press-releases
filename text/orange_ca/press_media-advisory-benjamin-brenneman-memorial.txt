MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will attend a tribute service for Benjamin Lee Brenneman in memoriam of the 30
th
anniversary of his murder. Brenneman was a 12-year-old newspaper delivery boy for the Orange County Register in 1981 when he was murdered on his delivery route by a recently-paroled child molester.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, Aug. 26, 2011, at 11:00 a.m.
WHERE: Modjeska Park, 1331 S. Nutwood Street, Anaheim, CA 92804

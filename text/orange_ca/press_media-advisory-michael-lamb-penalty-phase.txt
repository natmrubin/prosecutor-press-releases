MEDIA ADVISORY – MICHAEL LAMB PENALTY PHASE
For Immediate Release
Case # 03CF0441
August 20, 2007
Contact:
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney’s Office
WHAT:
Will announce a decision regarding the re-trial of the penalty phase against Michael Allen Lamb, 33.
Lamb was convicted in July of 2007 of murdering a fellow white supremacist gang member and attempting to murder a police officer.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Tuesday, August 21, 2007 at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-40,
Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
For more information on the trial of Lamb and co-defendant Jacob Rump, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select “Two White Supremacist Gang Members Convicted of Conspiring and Executing Fellow Gang Member for Appearing on TV” from July 11, 2007

THE ORANGE COUNTY DISTRICT ATTORNEY’S COMMUNITY OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENT AWARDS
District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will honor the efforts of 23 individuals that made outstanding contributions to public safety in 2004. Many of the honorees are witnesses or victims of crimes who went above and beyond the call of duty to help the Orange County District Attorney’s Office successfully prosecute a criminal case.
Time & Place: 3pm, February 25, 2005 in the First Floor Library, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, CA 92701.

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Hitomi Tsuyuki, 57, Coto de Caza
WHAT: Is expected to be sentenced tomorrow to 18 years in state prison for stealing approximately $2.8 million from vulnerable, elderly people, many of whom he met through his father’s church. Multiple victims are expected to attend the sentencing to give victim impact statements.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, Oct. 28, 2011, at 9:00 a.m.

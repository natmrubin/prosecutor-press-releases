For Immediate Release
Case # 13WF2319
July 31, 2013
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Illario Niko Johnson, 18
WHAT:
Is accused of participating in the disturbance in downtown Huntington Beach on Main Street that occurred July 28, 2013. Johnson is accused setting fire to a box of catalogs in the street and is charged with one felony count of arson of another’s property, and one misdemeanor count each of inciting riots, participating in a rout and unlawful assembly, and refusal to disperse. The People will be requesting bail
be set at $20,000 at his arraignment
. If convicted, he faces a sentencing ranging from probation up to three years in state prison.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Thursday, Aug. 1, 2013. The time is to be determined.
WHERE: West Justice Center, Westminster. The Department is to be determined.

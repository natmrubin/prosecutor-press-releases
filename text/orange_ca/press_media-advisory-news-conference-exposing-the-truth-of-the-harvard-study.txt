MEDIA ADVISORY – News Conference Exposing the Truth of the Harvard Study
Date: September 12, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference and attendees will be asked to present credentials
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and Assistant District Attorney Ebrahim Baytieh
WHAT: Will hold a news conference to present the findings of the OCDA Special Prosecutions Unit, who fact-checked a recent Harvard Law School-affiliated study called the Fair Punishment Project.
WHEN:
Tomorrow, Wednesday, Sept. 13, 2017, at 11:00 a.m.
Doors will open at 10:30 a.m. and embargoed copies of the news release and visuals will be made available for the press.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Watch remotely through Facebook Live at
https://facebook.com/OCDATony

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Zachary Joshua Reeder, 30, Orange
WHAT: Reeder is scheduled to be arraigned this afternoon. He is charged with 34 felony counts of distributing pornography to a minor, 34 felony counts of contacting a child with the intent to commit a lewd act, 34 felony counts of using a minor for sex acts, six felony counts of committing a lewd act upon a child, one felony count of control possession and control of child pornography, and one felony count of bringing obscene matter into California. Reeder’s maximum sentence is to be determined, but he faces mandatory lifetime sex offender registration if convicted. The People will be requesting the defendant be held on $3 million bail at his arraignment.
WHEN: This afternoon, Feb. 11, 2013. The time is to be determined.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
*Additional information about this case will be distributed in a press release at a later time. The investigation is ongoing. Anyone with additional information or who believes they have been a victim is encouraged to contact Supervising District Attorney Investigator Anthony Sosnowski at (714) 834-8794 or Irvine Police Department Detective Frough Jahid at (949) 724-7184.

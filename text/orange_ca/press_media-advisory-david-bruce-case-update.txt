Media Advisory – David Bruce case update
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Case # 15NF2656
Date: October 10, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: David Michael Bruce, 68, Anaheim
WHAT: Defendant Bruce was found deceased in his home this morning and the Orange County District Attorney’s Office dismissed the case against him. Bruce was facing eight felony counts of lewd acts upon a child under 14 and two felony counts of lewd acts on a child age 14 or 15, and closing arguments for his 11-day trial were scheduled to begin this morning.
WHEN: Today, Oct. 10, 2018.
Please contact Orange County Sheriff’s Department Public Information Officer Carrie Braun at 714-904-7042 with any questions not related to the criminal case against Bruce.

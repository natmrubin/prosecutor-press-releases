MEDIA ADVISORY- MISSION VIEJO CITY COUNCIL TO CONSIDER SEX OFFENDER ORDINANCE
For Immediate Release
October 17, 2011
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Senior Assistant District Attorney Mary Anne McCauley from the Orange County District Attorney’s Office
WHAT: Will attend the Mission Viejo City Council meeting in support of a City Ordinance to create a child safety zone in Mission Viejo parks to further protect children from registered sex offenders. The Council will discuss whether Mission Viejo should direct the City Attorney to model a “Child Safety Zone” City Ordinance similar to the County Ordinance proposed by Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Supervisor Shawn Nelson (Fourth District) and passed unanimously by the Orange County Board of Supervisors on April 5, 2011.
WHEN: Monday, Oct. 17, 2011, at 6:00 p.m.
WHERE: City Hall, 200 Civic Center, Mission Viejo
To read the full County Ordinance, please select the April 5, 2011, press release titled “Orange County Board of Supervisors Unanimously Votes to Adopt New Law to Keep Registered Sex Offenders Out of Parks, Harbors, Beaches and Playgrounds,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories at
www.orangecountyda.com
.
To read the agenda item, please select “View City Meeting Agenda,” under Residents on the homepage
https://cityofmissionviejo.org
. Click on Meetings tab and select the agenda for Oct. 17, 2011.

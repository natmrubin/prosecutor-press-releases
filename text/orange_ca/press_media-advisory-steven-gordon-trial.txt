MEDIA ADVISORY – Steven Gordon trial
Case #14ZF0337
Date: November 15, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Steven Dean Gordon, 47, Anaheim
WHAT: Will face trial for the special-circumstances murder of four women after kidnapping the victims. He is charged with four felony counts of special circumstances murder with sentencing enhancements for kidnapping and multiple murders. The Orange County District Attorney’s Office announced on Dec. 11, 2015, that it would seek the death penalty against Gordon.
WHEN: Opening statements are scheduled to begin tomorrow, Nov. 16, 2016, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-40, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Senior Deputy District Attorney Larry Yellin of the Homicide Unit is prosecuting this case.

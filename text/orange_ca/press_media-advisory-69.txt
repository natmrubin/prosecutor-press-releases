MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:     Former Irvine Police Officer David Alex Park, 29, of Lake Forest.
WHAT:  Arraignment on three counts of sexually assaulting a woman that he pulled over on a traffic violation in December 2004.
WHEN:   9 AM, Friday, March 25, 2005.
WHERE: Department C-55, Central Justice Center, 700 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana, CA 92701. Deputy District Attorney Steve McGreevy in the Sexual Assault Unit has been assigned to prosecute the case.

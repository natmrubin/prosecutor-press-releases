Media Advisory – DDA Jess Rodriguez statement
Date: February 9, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available on the matter
WHO: Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA)
WHAT: Has published a dismissal statement signed by Deputy District Attorney (DDA) Jess Rodriguez and Lake Forest Council Member Adam Nick. On Jan. 28, 2016, Adam Nick, who was previously prosecuted by DDA Rodriguez on behalf of OCDA, voluntarily dismissed with prejudice his civil lawsuit against DDA Rodriguez. The civil lawsuit was against DDA Rodriguez and not a lawsuit against OCDA or the county.
To read about
People v. Adam Nick
, please search the press release titled, “Lake Forest Councilman Charged with Illegally Removing Campaign Signs Belonging to Another Councilman,” on the OCDA website,
www.orangecountyda.org
.
WHEN: Today, Tuesday, Feb. 9, 2016
WHERE: The statement, “Dismissal Statement Signed by DDA Jess Rodriguez and Adam Nick,” is available at the OCDA website by selecting Reports under the Reports pull-down menu.

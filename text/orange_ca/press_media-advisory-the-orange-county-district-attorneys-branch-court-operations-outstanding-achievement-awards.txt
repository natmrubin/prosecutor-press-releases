MEDIA ADVISORY THE ORANGE COUNTY DISTRICT ATTORNEY’S BRANCH COURT OPERATIONS OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENT AWARDS
For Immediate Release
February 19th, 2004
Contact:
Mark Macaulay
(714) 347-8405 (O)
(714) 323-4486 (C)
Announcement
District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will honor the efforts of 18 individuals that made outstanding contributions to public safety in 2003. Many of the honorees are witnesses or victims of crimes who went above and beyond the call of duty to help the Orange County District Attorney’s Office successfully prosecute a criminal case.
Time & Place: 3pm, February 20, 2004 in the First Floor Library, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, Ca.

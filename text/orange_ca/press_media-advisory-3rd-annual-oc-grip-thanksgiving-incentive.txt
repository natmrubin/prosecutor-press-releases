MEDIA ADVISORY – 3RD ANNUAL OC GRIP THANKSGIVING INCENTIVE
For Immediate Release
November 14, 2012
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Media invited to attend the Turkey Dinner Giveaway today, November 14
WHO: Law enforcement and staff from Orange County Gang Reduction Intervention Partnership (OC GRIP) agencies and 475 at-risk students.
WHAT: Students who succeeded in a challenge to improve their attendance, behavior, and grades will pick up their turkey dinners in time for Thanksgiving at various participating OC GRIP schools.
WHEN: Today, Wednesday, Nov. 14, 2012, at 4:30 p.m.
Media interested in attending this event may contact Orange County District Attorney Spokesperson Farrah Emami at (714) 347-8405 for further information.
For more information on this event, please select the Nov. 9, 2012, press release titled “At-Risk Youth Earn 475 Turkey Dinners in Thanksgiving Gang Prevention Incentive,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories at
www.orangecountyda.com
.

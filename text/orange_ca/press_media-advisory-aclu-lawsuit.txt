MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU) v. Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and Orange Police Department (OPD) Chief Robert Gustafson
WHAT: The OCDA and OPD will defend a lawsuit against the OCDA filed by the ACLU.  The ACLU is suing to enjoin the OCDA from enforcing gang injunctions in defense of gang member rights.  Please check the OCDA website at
www.orangecountyda.com
for background on gang injunctions by typing “gang injunctions” in the search function.
WHEN: Beginning today, Monday, Nov. 8, 2010, at 2:30 p.m. and expected to last for approximately 20 court days.
WHERE: Courtroom 9, Federal Courthouse, 312 N. Spring Street, Los Angeles

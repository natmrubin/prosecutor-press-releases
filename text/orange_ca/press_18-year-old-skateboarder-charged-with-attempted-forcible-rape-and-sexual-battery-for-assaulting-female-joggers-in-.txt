SANTA ANA, Calif. – An 18-year-old man was charged with attempted forcible rape and sexual battery for assaulting female joggers in Irvine.

Circumstances of the Case
On Dec. 17, 2017, Ahmad is accused of groping Jane Doe 2’s buttocks as he rode past the jogger on his skateboard in Irvine. The victim later reported the incident to the Irvine Police Department (IPD).
On Dec. 18, 2017, the defendant is accused of riding his skateboard toward Jane Doe 1 and pushing her to the ground as she jogged.
Ahmad is further accused of covering the victim’s mouth and restraining her in an attempt to suppress her screams, then sexually assaulting her before fleeing the scene.
Jane Doe 1 reported the assault to IPD, who investigated this case.
On Jan. 19, 2018, Ahmad is accused of approaching Jane Doe 3 on his skateboard and grabbing the victim’s buttocks. Jane Doe 3 reported the sexual battery to IPD.
On Jan. 24, 2018, the defendant was arrested by IPD, who identified and located Ahmad near the scene of a reported sexual assault.
The Special Prosecutions Unit is prosecuting this case.

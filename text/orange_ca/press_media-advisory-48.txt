MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Gregory Haidl, Keith Spann, and Kyle Nachreiner charged with raping and sexually assaulting an unconscious 16-year-old female victim and videotaping the incident.
WHAT: Will be sentenced by Superior Court Judge Francisco Briseño.
WHEN: March 10, 2006, at 9:00 AM
WHERE: Department C45, Central Justice Center, 700 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, California  92701.
**Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Chief Assistant District Attorney Chuck Middleton will be available for comment following the sentencing at Central Justice Center’s second floor.

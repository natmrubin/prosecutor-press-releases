OCDA SEEKING POTENTIAL ADDITIONAL VICTIMS OF ATTORNEY ACCUSED OF SEXUALLY ASSAULTING WOMAN
SANTA ANA, Calif. – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) is seeking potential additional victims of an attorney accused of sexually assaulting a woman.
Defendant

Adam Rudnick Stull, 56, Ladera Ranch
Charged yesterday with the following counts:
Felony
Sexual battery by restraint

Sexual battery

Four years in state prison plus one year and five months in jail

Circumstances of the Case
At the time of the crimes, Stull was an active member of the State Bar of California and practicing law in Orange County. The defendant also worked as a deputy district attorney in Kern County in the 1990s.
On Nov. 7, 2017, the defendant is accused of restraining Jane Doe and touching her vagina and buttocks with sexual intent against the victim’s will.
On Nov. 20, 2017, the victim reported the incident to Orange County Sheriff’s Department, who investigated this case.
Law enforcement believes there may be additional victims. Anyone with additional information or who believes they may have been a victim is encouraged to contact OCDA Supervising Investigator Eric Wiseman at (714) 347-8794.

MEDIA ADVISORY – OCDA CHIEF OF STAFF SUSAN KANG SCHROEDER LIVE INTERVIEW ON KFI
For Immediate Release
July 17, 2013
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Chief of Staff Susan Kang Schroeder
WHAT: Will be speaking with KFI’s Bill Carroll this afternoon about the OCDA Human Exploitation and Trafficking (HEAT) Unit and discuss the concept behind publicizing the names of sex purchasers and their role in human exploitation and trafficking.
WHEN: Today, Wednesday, July 17, 2013, at 1:35 p.m.
WHERE: The Bill Carroll Show, radio station KFI AM 640
For more information about the most recent prosecution of sex purchasers, please visit the homepage at
www.orangecountyda.com
and select under Press Releases/Media Advisories the July 15, 2013, press release titled, “Eight Men Convicted and Sentenced for Soliciting Sex from Undercover Officer in Santa Ana Sting Operation.”
For more information about the HEAT Unit, please visit the homepage at
www.orangecountyda.com
and select under Press Releases/Media Advisories the April 26, 2013, press release titled, “OCDA to put their “HEAT” on Slave-Owner Perps.”

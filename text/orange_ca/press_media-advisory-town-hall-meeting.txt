MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: California State Senator Mimi Walters (R-Laguna Hills) and Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will hold a town hall meeting regarding identity theft, fraud, scams targeting senior citizens, and ways to avoid becoming a victim. The panel of speakers will include Assistant District Attorney Elizabeth Henderson of the Major Fraud Unit, Orange County Sheriff’s Economic Crimes Sergeant Rod Walker, TransUnion Department Manager of the Fraud Victim Assistance Program Valerie Smith, and Vice President of Government Relations for the California Bankers Association Alex Alanis.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, June 17, 2011, from 2:00 p.m. to 3:30 p.m.
WHERE: Clubhouse One, 24232 Calle Aragon, Laguna Woods
Media interested in attending are asked to RSVP to Senator Walters’ District Office at (949) 457-7333.
*Please Note:
This event is at capacity and is no longer accepting reservations from the public. Future dates for similar town hall presentations on this topic are will be announced soon.

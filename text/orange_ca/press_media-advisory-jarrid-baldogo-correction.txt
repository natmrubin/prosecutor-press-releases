MEDIA ADVISORY
CORRECTION: A press release issued May 13, 2013, by the Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) incorrectly stated that defendant Jarrid Thomas Baldogo, 29, Irvine, worked for the Woodbridge Homeowner’s Association in Irvine. Baldogo did not work for the Woodbridge Homeowner’s Association, rather he was hired to coach students at the tennis courts located on the Association’s property by a tennis professional who contracts with the Woodbridge Village Association.
Baldogo is charged with sexually assaulting a male student under the age of 14, whom he coached at the tennis courts located at Woodbridge Village Association in Irvine. To read the full press release, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the May 13, 2013, press release titled, “OCDA Seeks Public’s Help Identifying Potential Additional Victims of Tennis Coach Charged with Sexually Assaulting Male Student,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.
The online version of the press release reflects the corrected information regarding Baldogo’s employment.

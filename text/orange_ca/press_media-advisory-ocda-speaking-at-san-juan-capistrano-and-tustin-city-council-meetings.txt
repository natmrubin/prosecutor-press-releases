MEDIA ADVISORY – OCDA speaking at San Juan Capistrano and Tustin City Council meetings
Date: July 18, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT:
Will speak at San Juan Capistrano and Tustin City Council meetings tonight to inform citizens about the rising issue of human trafficking in Orange County. District Attorney Rackauckas will also share what OCDA is doing to combat this problem in our county.

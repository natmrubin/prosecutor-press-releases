For Immediate Release

June 7, 2013
Susan Kang Schroeder

Office: 714-347-8408

Farrah Emami

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Manuel Anthony Ramos, 39, and Jay Patrick Cicinelli, 41
WHAT: Appeared in court this morning for Cicinelli’s motion to sever hearing in the criminal case for their participation in the beating-death of a homeless man, Kelly Thomas. The severance motion was denied by the Honorable William R. Froeberg. A pre-trial was set for Ramos and Cicinelli, who will appear in Department C-40, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana, for their hearing on Aug. 30, 2013
.
A jury trial was set for Oct. 18, 2013.
To read the full press release on this case, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the link titled, “OCDA Charges Two Fullerton Police Officers for the Beating-Death of 37-Year-Old Homeless Man,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

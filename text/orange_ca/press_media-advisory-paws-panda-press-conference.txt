Media Advisory – PAWS PANDA press conference
Date: April 7, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will be hosting a press conference tomorrow to introduce the Pets Are Wonderful Support (PAWS) volunteers and therapy dogs of the newly launched PAWS Assist the Needs of the District Attorney (PANDA) program. The PANDA program will provide volunteers and their therapy dogs to the OCDA to comfort sexual assault victims as they meet with OCDA prosecutors to discuss case information and legal proceedings.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, April 8, 2016, at 11:00 a.m.
WHERE: First floor library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana

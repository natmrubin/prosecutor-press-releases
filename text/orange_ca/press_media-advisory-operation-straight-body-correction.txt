MEDIA ADVISORY- OPERATION STRAIGHT BODY CORRECTION
For Immediate Release
June 10, 2010
MEDIA ADVISORY
Please Note:
On June 3, 2010, the Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) released a press release titled “OCDA Charges 53 in Operation Straight Body Insurance Fraud Sting Targeting Auto Body Repair Facilities.”
Operation Straight Body was a 5-month undercover sting netting 53 defendants by the OCDA Automobile Insurance Fraud Unit targeting auto body repair facilities engaging in insurance fraud.
In the press release on June 3, 2010, the wrong business address was listed for Placentia Auto Body and defendant Mike Vargas Rocha, 54. The correct address is 229 Santa Fe Avenue in Placentia.

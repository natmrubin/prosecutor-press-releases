Media Advisory – Peter Chadwick profile
Date: December 9, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Peter Gregory Chadwick, 51
WHAT: Was profiled on the television show Crime Watch Daily. Peter Chadwick is accused of murdering his wife, Quee Chadwick and is currently a fugitive. The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) is seeking the public’s help in locating the defendant.
WHERE:
To view the two-part profile about Peter Chadwick, visit the following link:
https://crimewatchdaily.com/2015/12/07/multimillionaire-real-estate-mogul-wanted-for-murder-of-wife/
To read the OCDA’s press release about the case (Case # 12HF2960), please visit the following link:
https://ocdistrictattorney.gov/civica/press/display.asp?layout=2&Entry=4177

MEDIA ADVISORY – Insurance Fraud News Conference today
Date: June 5, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and California Department of Insurance
WHAT: Will hold a news conference today to announce the first phase of charges filed against 10 attorneys and six cappers for a multi-million dollar workers’ compensation insurance fraud scheme
WHEN:
Today, Monday, June 5, 2017, at 11:30 a.m.
Doors will open at 11:00 a.m. and embargoed copies of the news release, remarks, and visuals will be made available for the press.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Watch remotely and ask questions through Facebook Live at
https://www.facebook.com/OCDATony
Deputy District Attorney Noor Hasan of the Insurance Fraud Unit is prosecuting this case.

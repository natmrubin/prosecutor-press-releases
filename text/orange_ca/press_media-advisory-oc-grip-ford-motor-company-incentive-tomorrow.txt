Media Advisory – OC GRIP Ford Motor Company Incentive tomorrow
DATE: November 30, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Preview video featuring past events is viewable at the following link:
https://youtu.be/CfPVD9GBK28
WHO:
Ford Motor Company (Ford), RMD Group, Taco Bell, Orange County Gang Reduction Intervention Partnership and its partners including Buena Park Police Department, Fullerton Police Department, Garden Grove Police Department, Orange County Probation Department, Orange County Sheriff’s Department, Orange Police Department, Santa Ana Police Department, and Santa Ana Unified School District Police Department
WHAT:
Will reward 140 local elementary and middle school at-risk students with a visit to Ford’s Irvine design facilities as an incentive for positive behavior, perfect attendance, and staying out of gangs. To earn this reward, the students met a challenge to have no unexcused absences, criminal activity or arrests, violence or weapons on campus, and gang-related clothing, writing, or behavior.
WHEN: Friday, Dec. 1, 2017, at 8:30 a.m.
WHERE:
Ford Regional Headquarters, 3 Glen Bell Way #110, Irvine, CA 92618
Media interested in attending may contact Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Spokesperson Michelle Van Der Linden at (714) 347-8405.

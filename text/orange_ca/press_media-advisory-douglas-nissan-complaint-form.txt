September 26, 2008
Susan Kang Schroeder

Office: 714-347-8408

Farrah Emami

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHAT: People wishing to file a complaint against Douglas Nissan of Orange
HOW: Download or print the “Douglas Nissan Complaint Form” in English or Spanish at
www.orangecountyda.com
SUBMIT: Forms can be e-mailed to
ConsumerComplaint@da.ocgov.com
Faxed to (714)648-3636
Or mailed to:
Attn: Consumer Protection Unit
Orange County District Attorney’s Office
401 Civic Center Dr. W
Santa Ana, CA 92701
*Please retain a copy of the complaint for your records, as the original will not be returned. You will be contacted only if additional information is needed.

MEDIA ADVISORY- HEALTH AND SAFETY EXPO
For Immediate Release
April 13, 2009
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Capistrano
Unified School District, Orange County District Attorney’s Office,
and
Orange County Sheriff’s Department
WHAT: Will host a Health and Safety Expo, which is open to the public, to educate members of the community about law enforcement and school district safety programs. Local, State, and federal public officials are expected to attend, along with 45 exhibitors, who will have expo booths with information about programs available to students, parents, and community members.
WHEN: Wednesday, April 15, 2009, at 6:00 p.m.
WHERE:
Capistrano Unified School District’s Education Center, 33122 Valle Road, San Juan Capistrano

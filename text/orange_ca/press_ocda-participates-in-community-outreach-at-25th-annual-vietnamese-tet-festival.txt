OCDA PARTICIPATES IN COMMUNITY OUTREACH
AT 25TH ANNUAL VIETNAMESE TET FESTIVAL
SANTA ANA – The Government and Community Relations Unit of the Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) will be conducting community outreach activities at the 25th Annual TET Festival of Orange County, organized by the Union of Vietnamese Students Association.  The Opening Ceremony will begin tomorrow, Saturday, February 17, 2007, at 11:00 a.m. at Garden Grove Park in Garden Grove
District Attorney Tony Rackauckas, along with other government officials and community leaders, will attend the festival’s Opening Ceremony.
The OCDA Government and Community Relations Unit will staff two booths, F18 and F20, and will be handing out information and speaking with visitors about the negative consequences of driving under the influence.  In the spirit of the festivities, visitors may also win exciting prizes and gifts.

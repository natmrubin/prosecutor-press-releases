Media Advisory – Kathleen Roberts Los Angeles Superior Court appointment
Date: November 17, 2015
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Would like to congratulate Deputy District Attorney Kathleen Roberts on her appointment as a Orange County Superior Court judge. Kathleen Roberts is a resident of Los Angeles County and is currently assigned to the North Justice Center in Orange County.
“We are proud to have so many of our deputy district attorneys appointed and elected to the bench,” said District Attorney Rackauckas. “Appointments like this demonstrate the confidence and esteem that both the governor and the public have in the quality of work that our office provides. We wish Ms. Roberts well and much success in administering justice as a judge.”
WHEN: Today, Nov. 17, 2015

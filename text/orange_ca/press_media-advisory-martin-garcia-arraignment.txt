MEDIA ADVISORY – MARTIN GARCIA ARRAIGNMENT
Case # 16WF0796
Date: May 9, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Martin Rodriguez Garcia, 49, Las Vegas, Nevada
WHAT: Is scheduled to be arraigned on charges of one felony count of sexual intercourse or sodomy with a child 10 years or younger, one felony count of oral copulation or sexual penetration with a child 10 years or younger, 14 felony counts of lewd acts upon a child under 14, four felony counts of aggravated sexual assault of a child, and sentencing enhancement allegations for substantial sexual conduct with a child and committing a lewd act upon a child under 14 against multiple victims. If convicted, he faces a maximum sentence of 288 years and eight months to life in state prison. He is being held on $1 million bail.
WHEN: Today, May 9, 2016
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana. The time is to be determined.
Deputy District Attorney Bobby Taghavi of the Sexual Assault Unit is prosecuting this case.

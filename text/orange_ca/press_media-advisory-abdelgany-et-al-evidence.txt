MEDIA ADVISORY
WHAT: Evidence from the jury trial against 10 defendants convicted and sentenced for conspiracy to disrupt a meeting and speech by the Israeli Ambassador to the United States at the University of California, Irvine (UCI) is now available online. The evidence includes 28 e-mail threads outlining the conspiracy and cover-up, two video trial exhibits, and the People’s Opposition.
WHERE: To view the evidence, visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select “UCI Case Evidence” on the homepage under the Media Center.
To read the full press release on the conviction and sentencing in this case, visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the Sept. 23, 2011, press release titled “10 Men Convicted and Sentenced for Conspiracy and Disrupting a Meeting During Speech by Israeli Ambassador at UCI,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

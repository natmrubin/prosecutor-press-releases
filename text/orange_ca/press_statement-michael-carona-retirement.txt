Office: 714-347-8408

STATEMENT BY DISTRICT ATTORNEY
TONY RACKAUCKAS
“I believe today’s news of the Sheriff Michael Carona’s retirement
is good news for both him and the County.  The Orange County District
Attorney’s Office has enjoyed a good working relationship with
Assistant Sheriff Jack Anderson for many years.  Our latest major project was
the
South County gang injunctions.  We do not expect that today’s
news will have any effects in the day to day operations in our
departments working together.”

MEDIA ADVISORY – Steven Gordon death penalty verdict
Case #14ZF0337
Date: December 21, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Steven Dean Gordon, 47, Anaheim
WHAT: A jury recommended death today for Gordon in the special circumstances murder of four women. Gordon was convicted on Dec. 15, 2016, of four felony counts of special circumstances murder with sentencing enhancements for kidnapping and multiple murders.
WHEN: Gordon is scheduled to be sentenced on Feb 3, 2017, at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-40, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Senior Deputy District Attorney Larry Yellin of the Homicide Unit is prosecuting this case.

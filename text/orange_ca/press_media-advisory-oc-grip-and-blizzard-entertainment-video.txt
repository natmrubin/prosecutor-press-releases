MEDIA ADVISORY – OC GRIP AND BLIZZARD ENTERTAINMENT VIDEO
Date: June 14, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
SANTA ANA, Calif. – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office has posted a video showing staff from Blizzard Entertainment working with the Orange County Gang Reduction and Intervention Partnership to mentor students at Monte Vista Elementary School in Santa Ana.
To view the video, please visit the following link: OCDA YouTube Channel link:
https://youtu.be/VtzmVVf5Dbc
or OCDA Vimeo link:

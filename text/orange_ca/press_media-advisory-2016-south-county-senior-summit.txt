MEDIA ADVISORY – 2016 SOUTH COUNTY SENIOR SUMMIT
Date: May 19, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Senior Deputy District Attorney Elizabeth Henderson assigned to the Public Administrator
WHAT: Will be presenting information on senior fraud and abuse titled “Protecting Seniors and their Savings” at the 2016 South County Senior Summit. The theme of the event is “Aging Simplified: Orange County’s Commitment to Serving our Seniors.” This event will feature informative presentations specific to issues facing south county senior citizens, including information about local senior support offices and services.
WHEN: Friday, May 20, 2016, at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE: In Clubhouse 3, Laguna Woods Village, 23822 Avenida Sevilla, Laguna Woods
If you are interested in attending this event, please RSVP through the event’s EventBrite page using the following link: 2016southcountyseniorsummitrsvp.eventbrite.com
For more information about the event, you can contact the Orange County Office on Aging at 800-510-2020 or 714-480-6450.

For Immediate Release
August 6, 2014
ANNOUNCEMENT BY Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
SANTA ANA – Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas has announced the appointment of Farrah Emami as his Director of Public Affairs. Emami has been serving as the District Attorney’s Spokesperson since 2006. Before joining the Office, she received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication Studies from Loyola Marymount University.
The Director of Public Affairs position will perform the same duties as Spokesperson, assume new duties, and oversee the Public Affairs Unit, which includes Public Affairs Specialists Roxi Fyad and Chris Kim.

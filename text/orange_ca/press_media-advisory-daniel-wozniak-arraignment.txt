MEDIA ADVISORY – DANIEL WOZNIAK ARRAIGNMENT
For Immediate Release
Case # 10HF0920
June 1, 2010
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Daniel Patrick Wozniak, 26, Costa Mesa
WHAT: Will be arraigned on two felony counts of special circumstances murder for the murders of Samuel Herr and Juri Kibuishi. The special circumstance sentencing enhancements include multiple murders, murder for financial gain, and the personal discharge of a firearm causing death. If convicted, Wozniak faces a minimum sentence of life in state prison without the possibility of parole.
WHEN: Today, Tuesday, June 1, 2010, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE; Department CJ-1 Central Jail, Santa Ana

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHAT: Closing arguments in the jury trial against Mohamed Mohy-Eldeen Abdelgany, Khalid Gahgat Akari, Aslam Abbasi Akhtar, Joseph Tamim Haider, Taher Mutaz Herzallah, Shaheen Waleed Nassar, Mohammad Uns Qureashi, Ali Mohammad Sayeed, Osama Ahmen Shabaik, and Asaad Mohamedidris Traina.
The court’s pre-trial ruling regarding media requests will permit cameras in Department C-1 for closing arguments. Media planning to attend are asked to contact the court to confirm attendance.

WHERE: Department C-1, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

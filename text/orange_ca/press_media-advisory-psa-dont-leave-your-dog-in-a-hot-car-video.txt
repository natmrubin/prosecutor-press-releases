MEDIA ADVISORY PSA: Don’t Leave Your Dog in a Hot Car (Video)
Date: August 14, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas, Deputy District Attorney Jennifer Malone, and the Santa Ana Police Department
WHAT: Published a Public Service Announcement (PSA) on YouTube about the dangers of leaving your dog or pet unattended in a hot car. It’s not just cruel, it’s against the law.
Animal abuse can land you up to a year or more in jail and you can face serious fines. This summer, we want to remind everyone that even on a 78-degree day, the temperature inside a parked car can soar to between 100 and 120 degrees in just minutes.
To learn more and watch the PSA, please visit the following link:
https://youtu.be/fqpuSiAVdiE

MEDIA ADVISORY – JOSHUA BLOUNT ARRAIGNMENT
For Immediate Release
September 28, 2006
Contact:
Susan Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
(714) 347-8408 Office
(714) 292-2718 Cell
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Joshua Anthony Blount, 22
WHAT: To be arraigned on charges of murder with an enhancement for the personal discharge of a firearm causing death.  Blount is accused of murdering Israel Maciel in a drive-by shooting in Costa Mesa on August 2, 2006.
WHEN:
Today, September 28, 2006 at 1:30 p.m.
WHERE: Department H-2, Harbor Justice Center, Newport Beach.

MEDIA ADVISORY – JORGE CASTRO AND ALEJANDRO DE JESUS CRUZ
For Immediate Release
Case # 05ZF0118
October 4, 2007
Contact:
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Jorge Andres Castro, 31, Aliso Viejo, and Alejandro de Jesus Cruz, 35,
Miami
WHAT: Two former bail bond agents to be sentenced for conspiring to engage in attorney capping and an illegal attorney referral scheme. As co-owners of Xtreme Bail Bonds, Castro and Cruz acted as runners and cappers for attorney Joseph Gerard Cavallo and solicited business for him at Orange County jail facilities.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, October 5, 2007 at 8:30 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-36, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

District Attorney Introduces Vietnamese Outreach Program
For Immediate Release
June 19th, 2001
Contact: Thuy Linh (714) 347-8410
District Attorney Introduces Vietnamese Outreach Program
Santa Ana—District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will meet with key members and organizations of the Vietnamese community to introduce the District Attorney’s Vietnamese Outreach Program.  Mr. Rackauckas will promote the Office’s efforts towards educating the Vietnamese community about the criminal justice system and the role of the District Attorney’s Office in their community.
The Vietnamese Outreach Program is designed to enhance public perceptions of the District Attorney’s Office, local government and law enforcement in general.  It will educate the public on how to report crimes, assist in criminal investigations and cooperate in prosecutions resulting in a safer community in which to live and work.
The “Meet and Greet” event will be held at the Regent West Restaurant in Santa Ana on September 26th at 5:00 p.m.  District Attorney Tony Rackauckas will be available to answer questions and show support for the outreach program.  For more information, call the District Attorney’s Office Government & Community Relations Unit at (714) 347-8410.

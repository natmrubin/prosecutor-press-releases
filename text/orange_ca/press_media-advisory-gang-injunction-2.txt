MEDIA ADVISORY – GANG INJUNCTION
For Immediate Release
July 14, 2006
Contact:
Susan Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
(714) 347-8408 Office
(714) 323-4486 Cell
MEDIA ADVISORY
*A video of gang shootings and a large number of visuals will be
made available at the press conference
WHAT:
Hearing for a judge to sign an injunction against 134 members of a Santa Ana criminal street gang.
WHEN:
Friday, July 14, 2006 – Hearing at 8:00 a.m. and press conference at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE:
Injunction hearing will be held at the Central Justice Center, Department C1, 700 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA.
Press conference will be held at the Office of the District Attorney, Law Library, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA.
Please be advised that no one will be admitted to the press conference without presenting valid press credentials or a government or law enforcement ID.

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Annamaria Magno Gana, 41, unincorporated Tustin
WHAT: Will be arraigned for murdering her husband and attempting to murder her 16-year-old son by shooting them in the family’s Tustin Ranch home.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Wednesday, May 11, 2011. The time is to be determined.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana.
For additional details on the case, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the May 9, 2011, press release entitled “Woman Charged with Shoot-Murder of Husband and Attempted Murder of 16-Year-Old Son” on the homepage under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

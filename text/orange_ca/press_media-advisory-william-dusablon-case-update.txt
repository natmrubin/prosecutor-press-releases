Media Advisory – William Dusablon case update
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Case # 18NF3040
Date: November 15, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:   William Frederick Dusablon, 92, Fullerton
WHAT: Defendant Dusablon was found deceased in the hospital on Nov. 11, 2018, and the Orange County District Attorney’s Office dismissed the case against him. Dusablon was facing one felony count of murder with a sentencing enhancement of personal use of a deadly weapon.
WHEN: Yesterday, Nov. 14, 2018.
Please contact Orange County Sheriff’s Department Public Information Officer Carrie Braun at 714-904-7042 with any questions not related to the criminal case against Dusablon.

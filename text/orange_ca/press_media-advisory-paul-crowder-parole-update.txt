MEDIA ADVISORY – PAUL CROWDER PAROLE UPDATE
For Immediate Release
May 25, 2011
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: The Board of Parole Hearings, California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitations (Board)
WHAT: Due to an objection to a psychological report by Paul Crowder’s defense lawyer, the Board has postponed the parole hearing by a couple of months to an undetermined time. The inmate was convicted and sentenced to 19 years to life in state prison
for the 1991 shooting-murder of a 17-year-old high school student on prom night while she was sleeping in her hotel room.
WHEN: Today, May 25, 2011
WHERE: Deuel Vocational Institution, 23500 Kasson Rd., Tracy, CA
*For further information, please select the May 24, 2011, press release entitled “
OCDA to Oppose Parole of Man Who Murdered 17-Year-Old Student as She Slept on Prom Night in 1991
” under Press Releases/Media Advisories at
www.orangecountyda.com
.

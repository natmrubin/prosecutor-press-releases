For Immediate Release
November 16, 2007
Contact:
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHAT:
Hearings and press conference regarding the signing of gang injunctions
against members of two rival criminal street gangs from
San Juan
Capistrano  and
San Clemente.
EVENTS:
Today, Friday, November 16, 2007
Hearings
will be held at
8:00 a.m.
and
1:30 p.m.
in Department C-1, Central
Justice Center, 700 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA.
Press conference
will be held at
2:30 p.m.
in the Law Library of the Office
of the
District Attorney, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA.
Due to security issues, please be advised that no one will be admitted to the press conference without presenting valid press credentials or a government or law enforcement ID.

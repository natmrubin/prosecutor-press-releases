Case # 14HF1464
Date: March 21, 2018
MAN CONVICTED OF STABBING-MURDER OF VICTIM HE MET ON CRAIGSLIST
SANTA ANA, Calif. – A man was convicted today of stabbing and murdering a man he met on Craigslist
.
Defendant
Charges
Maximum Sentence
Court Date
Adam Anthony Ingala-Whiting, 24, Oceanside
Found guilty by a jury today of:
One felony count of second degree murder
Sentencing Enhancement
Personal use of a knife
16 years to life in state prison
Sentencing
May 18, 2018, 9:00 a.m.
Department C-41, Central Justice Center, Santa Ana
Circumstances of the Case:
On April 28, 2014, Ingala-Whiting responded to a Craigslist advertisement posted by the victim soliciting a sexual encounter.
Later that same evening, the defendant met 47-year-old Francesco Bonomolo at his home in Mission Viejo.
Sometime between April 28, 2014, and May 4, 2014, Ingala-Whiting murdered Bonomolo by stabbing him multiple times while in the victim’s home.
On May 4, 2014, an acquaintance went to Bonomolo’s home and discovered the victim’s body.
Prosecutor: Senior Deputy District Attorney Troy Pino, Homicide Unit

MEDIA ADVISORY- DANIEL WOZNIAK CASE UPDATE
For Immediate Release
Case # 10HF0920
June 10, 2010
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Costa Mesa Police Chief Chris Shawkey
WHAT: Will host a press conference to provide updates on the special circumstances murder case against Daniel Patrick Wozniak, 26, Costa Mesa. Wozniak is charged with murdering his neighbor for financial gain and murdering the victim’s friend in an effort to derail the investigation by framing the first victim
WHEN: Today, Thursday, June 10, 2010, at 11:00 a.m.
WHERE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office, first floor law library, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, CA 92701

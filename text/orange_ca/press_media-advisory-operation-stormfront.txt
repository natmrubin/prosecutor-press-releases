MEDIA ADVISORY – OPERATION STORMFRONT
For Immediate Release
December 16, 2010
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405
Cell: 714-323-4486
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives
California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation
Orange County District Attorney’s Office
Orange County Sheriff’s Department
United States Attorney’s Office
United States Secret Service
WHAT:
Will host a press conference to discuss the outcome of a multi-agency operation, Operation Stormfront, aimed at the take-down of numerous white supremacist prison and street gang criminal enterprises in Orange County.
WHEN:
Today, Thursday, Dec. 16, 2010, at 12:00 p.m.
WHERE:
First floor law library, Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Please Note: No additional information or interviews will be available
from any of the participating agencies until the press conference.

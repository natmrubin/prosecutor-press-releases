MEDIA ADVISORY: Critical Update Regarding Adult Involved in Garden Grove Basketball Attack on Teenager
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: December 9, 2021
PRESS RELEASE
Kimberly Edds
Public Information Officer
Office: 714-347-8405, Cell: 714-504-1917
media@da.ocgov.com
WHO:
Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer
WHAT:
Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer will host a call-in media availability to provide a critical update regarding an adult involved in the November 7, 2021 attack on a teenage basketball player in Garden Grove.
Per Welfare and Institutions Code Section 828, the District Attorney’s Office is prohibited from discussing anything related to juvenile investigations.
WHEN:
Thursday
,
December 9,
2021 at 10 a.m.
Members of the media please email
media@da.ocgov.com
for call-in information.

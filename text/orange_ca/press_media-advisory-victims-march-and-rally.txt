MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Will lead the first Orange County Victims’ Rights March and Rally. Attendance is estimated at approximately 400 people. Guest speakers will include:
·
Orange Police Chief Robert Gustafson, Vice President
of Orange County Chiefs’ of Police and Sheriff’s Association
·
Pat Bates, Board Chair Supervisor, 5
th
District, Orange County
·
Collene Campbell, sister of Mickey Thompson and mother of Scott Campbell, National Chair of Force 100, Founder and President of MOVE, “Memory Of Victims Everywhere”
·
Erin Runnion, mother of Samantha Runnion, Founder of The Joyful Child Foundation
·
Ruth Allen, widow of Philip Cousins, Parents Of Murdered Children Inc.
WHEN: Monday, April 27, 2009, 9:30 a.m.

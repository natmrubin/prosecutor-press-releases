MEDIA ADVISORY – ANTUNEZ, GARCIA, AND LEON OFFICER-INVOLVED SHOOTING INVESTIGATIONS
Date: May 17, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a letter containing the investigation findings and legal conclusions regarding the Costa Mesa Police Department’s officer-involved shooting of Vincent Leon. The full letter, “OCDA Report Officer-Involved Shooting Investigation – Vincent Leon,” is available at www.orangecountyda.org by selecting Officer-Involved Shootings and Custodial Death Letters under the Reports pull-down menu.
A letter regarding the Santa Ana Police Department’s officer-involved shooting of Armondo Antunez and Garden Grove Police Department’s officer-involved shooting of Andrew Garcia have also been published. The letter, “OCDA Report Officer-Involved Shooting Investigation – Armondo Artunez,” and “OCDA Report Officer-Involved Shooting Investigation – Andrew Garcia,” are available at www.orangecountyda.org by selecting Officer-Involved Shootings and Custodial Death Letters under the Reports pull-down menu.
WHEN: Today, May 17, 2016

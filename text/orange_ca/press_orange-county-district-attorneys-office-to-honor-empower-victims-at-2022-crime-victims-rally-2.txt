Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer to Announce National Public Service Announcement Campaign to Combat Illegal Street Racing
Fast & Furious Actors Sung Kang and Cody Walker Help Warn Drivers of the Dangers
of Illegal Street Racing, 
Urge Car Exhibitionists to Keep it on the Track
WHO
:   Orange County District Attorney Todd Spitzer, actor Sung Kang of
Fast & Furious media franchise, Pat Harbrecht, widow of veteran Orange County Register editor Gene Harbrecht, who was killed in an illegal street race in 2020, Lili Trujillo Puckett, who founded Street Racing Kills in 2014 after her 16-year-old daughter Valentina was killed by a young man who was street racing and crashed after giving her a ride home, and California Highway Patrol Westminster Area Commander Denise Soffa.
WHAT
:
The debut of a nationwide anti-street racing public service announcement campaign featuring members of the Fast & Furious media franchise
WHEN: Friday, April 29 at 10 a.m.
WHERE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office – roof of the parking structure
300 N. Flower Street,
Santa Ana, CA 92703
Credentialed media will be allowed to set up beginning at 9:30 a.m.

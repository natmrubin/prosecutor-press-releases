For Immediate Release
Case # 11WF1668
July 22, 2011
Susan Kang Schroeder
Chief of Staff
Office: 714-347-8408

Farrah Emami

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Catherine Kieu, 48, Garden Grove
WHAT: Will appear in court this morning for a scheduled arraignment,
where the defense is expected to request a continuance
. Kieu is charged with one felony count of torture and one felony count of aggravated mayhem with sentencing enhancements for great bodily injury and personal use of a knife. If convicted on all counts, she faces a maximum sentence of life in state prison with the possibility of parole. The defendant is being held without bail.
WHEN: Today, Friday, July 22, 2011, at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE:
Department W-15
, West Justice Center, Westminster
*For further information, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select under Press Releases/Media Advisories the July 13, 2011, Orange County District Attorney Press Release titled “Woman to be Arraigned for Cutting off Man’s Penis After Tying Him to Bed.”

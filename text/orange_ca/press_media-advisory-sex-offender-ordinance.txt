For Immediate Release

Susan Kang Schroeder

Farrah Emami

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: The Orange County Board of Supervisors
WHAT: Will consider a second reading and adoption of the County Ordinance (Agenda item #11-000474) proposed by Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas and Supervisor Shawn Nelson (Fourth District). This Ordinance creates a child safety zone to further protect children from registered sex offenders and makes it a misdemeanor for registered sex offenders to enter County recreational areas where children regularly gather.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Tuesday, April 5, 2011, beginning at 9:30 a.m.
WHERE: Orange County Hall of Administration, 10 Civic Center Plaza, Santa Ana, CA 92701
For additional information on the proposed Ordinance, please visit
www.orangecountyda.com
and select the March 16, 2011, press release entitled “Orange County Proposed New Law to Keep Registered Sex Offenders Out of Parks and Playgrounds,” under Press Releases/Media Advisories.

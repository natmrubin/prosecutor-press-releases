MEDIA ADVISORY – ALEJANDRO RUIZ CHARGES
Case # 16CF0074
Date: January 12, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Alejandro Guerrero Ruiz, 23, Orange
WHAT: Was charged today with four felony counts of murder with special circumstance sentencing enhancements for multiple murders and murder during the commission of a robbery. If convicted, he faces a minimum sentence of life in state prison without the possibility of parole.
The defendant is currently awaiting extradition from Texas to Orange County. The investigation in this case is ongoing. A booking photo of the defendant and any additional information will not be released at this time.

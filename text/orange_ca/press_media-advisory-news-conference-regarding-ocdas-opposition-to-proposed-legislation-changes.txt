MEDIA ADVISORY – News Conference regarding OCDA’s opposition to proposed legislation changes
Date: July 7, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas, Dr. Henry T. Nicholas III (Marsy’s Law), and Erin Runnion (The Joyful Child Foundation)
WHAT: Will hold a news conference to discuss strong opposition to three California State Senate bills currently pending in the Assembly that will make dramatic, negative changes to firearm sentencing enhancements, the bail system/ pretrial release, and sex offender registration.
WHEN:
Monday, July 10, 2017, at 11:00 a.m.
Doors will open at 10:00 a.m. and a prosecutor will present a thorough overview of the proposed legislation to provide background prior to the on-record portion of the event. The District Attorney’s remarks will begin at 11:00 a.m. Embargoed copies of the news release, remarks, and visuals will be made available for the press.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
To read the proposed legislation in their entirety, please see the links below:
SB 10: Bail –
https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=201720180SB10
SB 421: Sex offender registration –
https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=201720180SB421
SB 620: Firearm enhancements –
https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=201720180SB620

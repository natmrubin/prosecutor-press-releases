MEDIA ADVISORY – 1994 Cold Case Arrest and Charges news conference
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Date: November 2, 2017
MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the news conference
WHO:
Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas and Newport Beach Police Department Chief Jon Lewis
WHAT:
Will hold a news conference to announce the arrest and charges filed against the suspect in a 1994 cold case murder.
WHEN: Thursday, Nov. 2, 2017, at 1:00 p.m.
Doors will open at 12:30 p.m. for credentialed members of the press, OCDA staff, and invited guests.
WHERE:
First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana
Watch remotely through Facebook Live at
https://facebook.com/OCDATo

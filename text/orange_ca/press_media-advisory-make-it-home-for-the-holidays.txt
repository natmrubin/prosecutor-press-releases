MEDIA ADVISORY – “MAKE IT HOME FOR THE HOLIDAYS”
For Immediate Release
December 11, 2006
Contact:
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
(714) 347-8405 Office
(714) 323-4486 Cell
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
Riverside County District Attorney-elect Rod Pacheco
San Bernardino County District Attorney Mike Ramos
WHAT: The Orange County, Riverside County, and San Bernardino County District Attorneys, in conjunction with the California District Attorney’s Association and prosecutors from around the state, will hold a news conference to discuss “Make it Home for the Holidays,” a new program targeting impaired drivers.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Tuesday, December 12, 2006 at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Riverside County District Attorney’s Office
4075 Main Street, Riverside, CA 92501
For more information contact
Farrah Emami
, Orange County District Attorney’s Office, (714) 347-8405,
Ingrid Wyatt
, Riverside County District Attorney’s Office, (951) 955 5626, or
Susan Mickey
, San Bernardino County District Attorney’s Office, (909) 387-6856.

Media Advisory – Video of Grant Robicheaux and Cerissa Riley news conference
Case # 18HF1291
Date: September 18, 2018
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:   Orange County District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT: Published a video of today’s news conference announcing charges and seeking potential additional victims of
Grant William Robicheaux and Cerissa Laura Riley
. The defendants are accused of sexually assaulting two women by use of drugs on separate occasions in Newport Beach.
Update: The arraignment for the defendants has been scheduled for
Oct. 25, 2018
, at 8:30 a.m. in Department H-7, Harbor Justice Center, Newport Beach. A previous news release stated Oct. 24, 2018.
To watch the video, please visit the following link:
https://youtu.be/xRx4IBvvT30

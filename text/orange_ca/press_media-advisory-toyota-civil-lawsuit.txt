For Immediate Release
March 12, 2010
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Orange County District Attorney and others
WHAT: Will announce at a press conference the filing of a civil lawsuit against Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A., Inc. to enjoin them from continuing to endanger the public through the sale of defective vehicles and deceptive business practices.
WHEN: Today, Friday, March 12, 2010, at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE: Orange County District Attorney’s Office, first floor law library, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana, CA 92701

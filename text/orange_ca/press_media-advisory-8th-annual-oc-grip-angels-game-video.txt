MEDIA ADVISORY – 8TH ANNUAL OC GRIP ANGELS GAME VIDEO
Date: June 15, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
SANTA ANA, Calif. – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) has posted a video showing highlights from the 8th Annual Orange County Gang Reduction Intervention Partnership (OC GRIP) Angels game as a reward for students in the program who improved school attendance and behavior and for staying out of gangs.
To read more about this year’s program or to learn more about OC GRIP, please visit the OCDA website, click on the Press Releases and Media Advisories tab under Media Center, and search for the press release titled, “1,900 Students Hit 8th Annual Angels Anti-Gang Challenge Out of the Ballpark.”
To view the video, please visit the following link: OCDA YouTube Channel link:
https://youtu.be/lphM1rWrOe0
or OCDA Vimeo link:
https://vimeo.com/170859502

MEDIA ADVISORY – ALLAWAY STATE HOSPITAL TRANSFER
Date: August 19, 2016
MEDIA ADVISORY
*No further information will be released prior to the press conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas; Pat Almazan, daughter of mass-murder victim Frank Teplansky; and Paul Paulsen, brother of mass-murder victim Deborah Paulsen.
WHAT: Will hold a press conference this morning to discuss the state hospital transfer of criminally insane mass murderer Edward Charles Allaway, 77, who worked as a custodian at California State University, Fullerton, and on July 12, 1976, opened fire and shot nine people, murdering seven.
WHEN: Today, Friday, Aug. 19, 2016, at 11:00 a.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Sheila Marie Sikat, 28, Rancho Santa Margarita
WHAT: To be sentenced for repeatedly molesting a 4-year-old girl with her husband in their Rancho Santa Margarita home and video taping almost four hours of the graphic sexual assault. Sikat was convicted December 18, 2007 of 55 counts of sexually assaulting a child. Sikat’s husband, David Hwang,
pleaded guilty on October 11, 2006 to 60 counts of various child molest charges and was sentenced to 50 years to life in prison.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Friday, February 22, 2008 at
8:30 a.m.
WHERE: Department C-37,
Central Justice Center, Santa Ana

MEDIA ADVISORY – PAUL CURRY ARRAIGNMENT
For Immediate Release
Case # 10CF3053
December 3, 2010
Susan Kang Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
Office: 714-347-8408
Cell: 714-292-2718
Farrah Emami
Spokesperson
Office: 714-347-8405

MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO: Paul Marshal Curry, 54, Salina, KS
WHAT: Will be arraigned on one felony count of special circumstances murder for financial gain with a sentencing enhancement for murder by poisoning. If convicted, the defendant faces a minimum sentence of life in state prison without the possibility of parole. Curry was arrested in Kansas Nov. 9, 2010, for the 1994 poisoning-murder of his wife in San Clemente and was extradited back to Orange County yesterday, Thursday, Dec. 2, 2010.
The full press release with additional information on this case titled, “Husband Arrested in Kansas for 1994 Poisoning-Murder for Financial Gain of Wife in San Clemente,” can be accessed at www.orangecountyda.com under Press Releases/Media Advisories published in November 2010.
WHEN: Monday, Dec. 6, 2010, at 9:00 a.m.
WHERE: Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana

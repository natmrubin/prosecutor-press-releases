OCDA SEEKS PUBLIC’S HELP IDENTIFYING POTENTIAL ADDITIONAL VICTIMS OF MAN WHO SEXUALLY ASSAULTED CHILDREN UNDER 14
SANTA ANA, Calif. – The Orange County District Attorney’s Office (OCDA) is seeking the public’s help identifying potential additional victims of a man who sexually assaulted two victims under the age of 14.
Defendant
Charges
Court Date
Victor Norman Ealey, 56, Garden Grove
Charged on May 22, 2017, with:
Two felony counts of lewd acts on a child under 14
Sentencing Enhancement:
Multiple victims
Continued Arraignment
June 6, 2017, 10:00 a.m.
Department CJ-1, Central Jail, Santa Ana
Bail: $1 million
Circumstances of the Case
At the time of the crime, Ealey was in a position of trust and had access to minor children.
On or about April 30, 2017, the defendant is accused of committing lewd acts upon 10-year-old Jane Doe 1, who reported the assault to Garden Grove Police Department (GGPD).
During GGPD’s investigation, Jane Doe 2 reported she was also a victim of the defendant.
Between Jan. 1, 2014 and Dec. 31, 2016, Ealey is accused of committing lewd acts upon Jane Doe 2, who was approximately 10 years old at the time of the crime.
The investigation in this case is ongoing. Anyone with additional information or who believes they may have been a victim is encouraged to contact OCDA Supervising District Attorney Investigator Eric Wiseman at (714) 347-8794.
Deputy District Attorney Lexie Elliot of the Sexual Assault Unit is prosecuting this case.

MEETING ADVISORY
WHO:
Orange County District Attorney
and
Korean American Advisory Commissioners
WHAT:
The District Attorney
will
hold a special Korean American Advisory Commission meeting to discuss the factual and legal findings of the investigation regarding the Michael Cho shooting.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Thursday, June 26, 2008, at 10:00 a.m. Please RSVP to
Roxi.Sotomayor@da.ocgov.com
WHERE: First floor law library of the Orange County District Attorney’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive West, Santa Ana 92701
Please be advised: This is not a press conference. Members of the media are invited as a courtesy to observe the meeting and are not permitted to video or audio record while the meeting is in session. Recorded interviews may be conducted with individual participants after the meeting has adjourned.

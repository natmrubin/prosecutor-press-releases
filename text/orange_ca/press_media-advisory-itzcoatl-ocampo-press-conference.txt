MEDIA ADVISORY
*Please note: No additional information or interviews will be available prior to the press conference
WHO: Orange County District Attorney (OCDA) Tony Rackauckas, and representatives from the Federal Bureau of Investigation, Orange County Sheriff’s Department, Anaheim Police Department, Placentia Police Department, and Brea/Yorba Linda Police Department.
WHAT: Will hold a press conference tomorrow to discuss a filing decision and arraignment information for
Itzcoatl Ocampo, accused of
the
serial murder-spree of four homeless men
.
WHEN: Tomorrow, Tuesday, Jan. 17, 2012, at 11:00 a.m.
WHERE: First floor law library, OCDA’s Office, 401 Civic Center Drive W., Santa Ana

MEDIA ADVISORY – SKYLAR DELEON PRESS CONFERENCE
For Immediate Release
September 5, 2006
Contact:
Susan Schroeder
Public Affairs Counsel
(714) 347-8408 Office
(714) 323-4486 Cell
MEDIA ADVISORY
WHO:
District Attorney Tony Rackauckas
WHAT:
Press conference to discuss updates regarding the 2004 murder of Jackie and Thomas Hawks in Newport Beach.  Defendants are Skylar Deleon, his wife Jennifer Deleon, and John Fitzgerald Kennedy.
WHEN:
Wednesday, September 6, 2006 at 10:00 a.m.
WHERE:
Office of the District Attorney, Law Library, 401 Civic Center Drive, Santa Ana, CA.
